Downloading a QR Code

You can see and download the unique QR code of a campaign in the All Campaigns page.
  1. In the Home page, click Home, and then click All Campaigns.
    The All Campaigns page is displayed.
  2. In the Your campaigns section, select the appropriate campaign, and click .
    Note:
    The Show QR Code option is not available for the campaigns in DRAFT status.
  3. From the drop-down list, select Show QR Code.
    The QR code is displayed.
  4. To download the QR code as an image, click .
    The QR code is downloaded as an image.
  5. To download the QR code as a PDF, click .
    The QR code is downloaded as a PDF.
