Closing a Campaign

The campaign is in COMPLETED status.
You can close a campaign in the All Campaigns page.
  1. In the Home page, click Home, and then click All Campaigns.
    The All Campaigns page is displayed.
  2. In the Your campaigns section, select the appropriate campaign in COMPLETED status, and click .
  3. From the drop-down list, select Close Campaign.
    A confirmation dialog box is displayed.
  4. Click Yes.
    The campaign is closed successfully, and the status is changed to CLOSED status.
