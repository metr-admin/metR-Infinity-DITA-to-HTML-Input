General Settings

The General Settings section enables you to update the agency name and add a logo to the campaign.
  1. In the Home page, click Settings and then select Application Settings.
    The Application Settings page is displayed.
  2. In the General Settings section, perform the following steps:
    1. In the AGENCY NAME field, enter the name of the agency.
    2. In the EMAIL field, enter the name of the email address.
    3. In the LOGO field, click Add Logo to browse and select the logo.
  3. Click Save.
