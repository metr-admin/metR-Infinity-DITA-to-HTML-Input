Managing a Template

You can manage the public portal template by changing the layout, logo, and header.
  1. In the Home page, click Settings, and then select Public Portal Template.
    The Public Portal Settings page is displayed.
  2. In the CHOOSE LAYOUT section, select a layout or theme for the portal.
  3. To add a logo, in the LOGO section, click Add Logo to browse and select the appropriate logo.
  4. In the ADD HEADER TITLE section, enter the title for the public portal.
