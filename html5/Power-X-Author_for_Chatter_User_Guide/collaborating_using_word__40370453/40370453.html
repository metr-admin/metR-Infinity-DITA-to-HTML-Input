Collaborating Using Word

Working in Word, you can add comments to your Chatter feed or share whole documents.

Parent topic: Sharing Options

To Collaborate Using Word

  1. Create a new document or open an existing document in Word.
  2. Connect to X-Author Chatter as described in Connecting to X-Author Chatter Online.
  3. Select who you want to collaborate with in your Chatter feed by clicking one of the icons:

    /Chatter icon/

    Your audience will be everyone in your organization with a Chatter account.

    /Group icon/

    Your audience will be all the members of the group you select.

    /Individual icon/

    Your audience will be the single individual you select.
  4. Make changes to the document using Word and then choose what content to share.

    /screenshot of chatter window and drop-down/

    Post

    Comments you provide in Chatter are included, but no part of the document is included.

    Selected Content

    The content highlighted in your document is inserted into the Chatter feed, as unformatted text. There is a maximum of 1000 characters.

    (Not currently supported. Choosing this option shares your Comments to the feed and the entire document is attached to the Chatter feed.)

    Entire Document

    The document is attached to the feed.

    Selected Content + Entire Document

    Highlighted content (up to 1000 characters) and the document (as an attachment) are included in the feed.

    (Not currently supported. Choosing this option shares your Comments to the feed and the entire document is attached to the Chatter feed.)
  5. Click Share to share your content to the Chatter feed.
