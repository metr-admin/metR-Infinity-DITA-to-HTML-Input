Connecting to X-Author Chatter Online

This page describes the following steps for connecting to X-Author Chatter Online:

  1. Launch the X-Author Chatter App
  2. Log in to Microsoft Office.
  3. Load the X-Author Chatter add-in.
  4. Connect to Salesforce.
Parent topic: X-Author Chatter Online June 2016 User Guide

To Launch X-Author Chatter Online and Load the Add-In

  1. Open the following link in your browser: https://xauthor.cloudapp.net/XAChatterOnline. You will see the following screen:/screenshot/
  2. Open the following link in a new tab: https://xauthor.sharepoint.com/sites/xatest. The Microsoft Office 365 sign-in screen is displayed./screenshot/
  3. Enter login information for your Microsoft Office account and click Sign in. The app catalog page is displayed.
  4. Click on the grid button in the upper-left-hand corner of the page and select the application you want to use (Excel is used in this example).
  5. Create a new document or open an existing document.
  6. From the Insert menu, click the Office Add-ins button./screenshot/
  7. From the My Organizations tab, choose the Ink (Chatter) add-in and click Insert. The X-Author Chatter task pane is displayed to the right of your document./screenshot/
  8. From the task pane, click Connect to Salesforce.
  9. Enter your Salesforce login credentials and click Log In. After successful login the task pane refreshes to display your default Chatter feed. You are ready to use X-Author Chatter with your document.
