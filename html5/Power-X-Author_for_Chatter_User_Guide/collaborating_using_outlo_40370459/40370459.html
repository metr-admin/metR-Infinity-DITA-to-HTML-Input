Collaborating Using Outlook

Outlook provides the ability to enter comments into Chatter to solicit feedback. This version of X-Author Chatter Online does not yet support email content or attachment sharing to Chatter.

Parent topic: Sharing Options

To Collaborate Using Outlook

  1. Create a new email or open an existing email.
  2. Load the X-Author Chatter Online add-in by doing one of the following:
    1. If you are creating a new email message, click the Add-ins icon at the top of the message window and choose Ink Outlook Online from the task pane to the right. X-Author opens inside the task pane.

      /screenshot/

    2. If you are opening an existing email, click the Ink Outlook Online app link just below the recipient line of the email message. X-Author opens in a separate pane below the link.

      /screenshot/

  3. Connect to X-Author Chatter Online as described in Connecting to X-Author Chatter Online.
  4. Select who you want to collaborate with in your Chatter feed by clicking one of the icons:

    /Chatter icon/

    Your audience will be everyone in your organization with a Chatter account.

    /Group icon/

    Your audience will be all the members of the group you select.

    /Individual icon/

    Your audience will be the single individual you select.
  5. Choose how you want to share content to Chatter.

    /screenshot of chatter window and drop-down/

    Post

    Enter a comment to solicit feedback, while not copying any content from the body of the email or email attachments.

    Selected Content

    Not supported.

    Selected Slides

    Not supported.

    Entire Presentation

    Not supported.

  6. Click Share to add your comments to the Chatter feed.
