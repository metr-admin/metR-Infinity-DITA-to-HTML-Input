Collaborating Using Excel

Working in Excel, you can add comments to your Chatter feed or share whole workbooks.

Parent topic: Sharing Options

To Collaborate Using Excel

  1. Create a new workbook or open an existing workbook in Word.
  2. Connect to X-Author Chatter as described in Connecting to X-Author Chatter Online.
  3. Select who you want to collaborate with in your Chatter feed by clicking one of the icons:

    /Chatter icon/

    Your audience will be everyone in your organization with a Chatter account.

    /Group icon/

    Your audience will be all the members of the group you select.

    /Individual icon/

    Your audience will be the single individual you select.
  4. Make changes to the workbook using Excel and then choose what content to share.

    /screenshot of chatter window and drop-down/

    Post

    Comments you provide in Chatter are included, but no part of the workbook is included.

    Selected Content

    The selected content is exported and attached to the Chatter feed as a new xls file. All selected content must be in adjoining cells. The minimum content that can be selected is a whole cell. Selected text within a cell cannot be shared.

    (Not currently supported. Choosing this option shares your Comments to the feed and the entire workbook is attached to the Chatter feed.)

    Active Worksheet

    The entire worksheet is copied and attached to the feed as a new xls file.

    (Not currently supported. Choosing this option shares your Comments to the feed and the entire workbook is attached to the Chatter feed.)

    Entire Workbook

    The whole workbook is attached to the feed.
  5. Click Share to share your content to the Chatter feed.
