Forklift and Pallet Truck Transportation

After the bottom mounted IP kit(s) have been removed, move the transport unit to the installation location with a forklift or pallet truck.

Before transportation check that:

  • The forks are placed as far apart as possible under the transport unit.

  • The frame of the transport unit rests completely on the forks.

  • The distance between the forks and center of gravity (A) is approximately equal so that the center of gravity is in the middle of the forks.

  • The forks extend past the back of the system (B).
