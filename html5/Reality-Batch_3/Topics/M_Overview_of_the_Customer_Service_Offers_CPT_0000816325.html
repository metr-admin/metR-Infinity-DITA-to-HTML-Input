Overview of the Customer Service Offers

Reality services makes it easier for you to operate, install and maintain your system. There are two types of service offers:

  • On demand services: Offers a range of services at a pre-determined cost or hourly rate.

  • Service contracts: Offers services agreements to meet your specific needs, with a yearly fee and pre-agreed upon rates. This makes it easier for proactive budgeting and service planning.

    Note: A service contract is a formal, mutually-agreed-upon, binding contract between Schneider Electric and a client. In the contract, we make a commitment to provide services over a fixed time period, and at fixed rates or a yearly fee.
