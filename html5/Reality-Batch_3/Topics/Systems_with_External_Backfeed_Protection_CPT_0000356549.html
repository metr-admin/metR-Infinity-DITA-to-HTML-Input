External Backfeed Protection

DANGER
HAZARD OF ELECTRIC SHOCK, EXPLOSION, OR ARC FLASH
In systems without internal backfeed protection, the bypass mains supply connection must be provided with a suitable isolator of the type MCCB by the customer, according to IEC/EN 60947â€“2 or UL 489/CSA C22.2 No. 5 (depending on which apply to your local area).
Note: The isolator used for the external backfeed protection must be rated according to the bypass on the rating plate.
Besides installing the external backfeed protection, warning labels must also be placed:
  • on the system at the bypass input terminal by the supplier
  • on the power isolator (the external backfeed protection) installed upstream of the bypass input terminal by the customer

The warning label should contain the following text (or equivalent in language(s) which are acceptable in the country in which the system is installed):

DANGER
Hazard of voltage backfeed
Before working on this circuit:
  • Isolate the uninterruptible power supply (UPS) System
  • Check for hazardous voltage between all terminals, including the protective earth
