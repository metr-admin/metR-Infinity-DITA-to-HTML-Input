How to Turn On the System

Note
HIGH CURRENT FLOW
Only close the rectifier breaker when the internal bus has been energized by the bypass.

Prerequisites:

  • The system has been commissioned.

  • The batteries have been commissioned and are ready to be connected.

  • The rectifier mains is available and within tolerance.

  • The bypass mains is available and within tolerance.

Note: Read the complete procedure before you turn on the system.
  1. Check that all main breakers are open and that the load is disconnected.
  2. Close the BreakersPB_xx051 to energize the internal supply.
  3. The Display starts and after a few moments and shows the Home screen.
    Note: If active, turn off the audible buzzer by pressing the Mute icon in the upper right corner of the Display. The Mute icon is only visible if the audible buzzer is active.
  4. Close the BreakersPB_xx001 to turn on the power supply to the rectifier.
  5. Press the Switch operation icon in the Status bar.
  6. Press the Switch system on button. Confirm the change.
  7. Check that the DC voltage is in range. If the DC voltage is within tolerance, close the BreakersPB_xx004.
  8. If the output is ready to be energized, close the BreakersPB_xx052.
  9. If available, close any output feeders.
  10. Check that the system is in normal operation and that there are no active warnings or alarms on the Display.
The system is now turned on and the system output is energized.
