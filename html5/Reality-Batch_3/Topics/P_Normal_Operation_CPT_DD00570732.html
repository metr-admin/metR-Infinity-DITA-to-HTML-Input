Normal Operation and Forced Inverter Operation

If the Unit state is Online mode, the system can be either in normal operation or forced inverter operation.

Note: The Unit state is indicated in the bottom left corner of the Display.

In normal operation the rectifier supplies the inverter and charges the battery. The inverter is synced to the bypass and supplies the load. The bypass mains is available and the static switch is energized. All breakers except the manual bypass are closed.

Charge Modes

When the rectifier is in operation, the rectifier might be in different charge modes depending on the battery charge current or in case the charge mode has manually been changed.

Mainly relevant for the unit state Online mode

There different charge modes are:

  • Float charge, default charge mode.

  • Boost charge, used if allowed charge the batteries faster with a higher voltage.

  • Initial charge, used if allowed to charge the batteries with a higher voltage during commissioning or maintenance.

The defined levels for float, boost and initial charge can be found in the Technical Data.
