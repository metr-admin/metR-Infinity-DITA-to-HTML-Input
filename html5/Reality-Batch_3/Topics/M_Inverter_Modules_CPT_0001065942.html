Inverter Modules

The inverter module converts DC (input) to AC (output). The module is fan cooled.

The input DC voltage settings depends on the DC input source. The output AC voltage depends on the system configuration.

Different modules are available for the different DC inputs and AC outputs combinations.

Input DC voltage Output AC voltage
24, 48 or 60 V DC 220, 230, or 240 V AC
110/125 V DC
220 V DC
24, 48 or 60 V DC 110, 120 or 127 V AC
110/125 V DC
Note: The 24, 48 and 60 V DC input voltage inverter modules have DIP switches on the back that sets the correct DC voltage. For more information see DIP-Switches for Inverter Module Input Setting.
