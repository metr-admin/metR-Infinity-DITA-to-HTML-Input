Cable Inlets

DANGER
HAZARD OF ELECTRIC SHOCK, EXPLOSION, OR ARC FLASH
  • Never drill or cut holes for cables or conduits with the gland plates installed.
  • Never drill or cut holes near the system.

Remove the gland plates and drill the required holes for the cables connecting to the terminals in the system.

The cable entry for the system can either be from the bottom, the top or both.

Drill the holes in the gland plates so that the connecting cables can be as straight as possible when attached to the terminals.
