2N Configuration with One of the Rectifier Mains Unavailable

If one of the rectifier mains becomes unavailable in a system with a 2N configuration, the other rectifier mains will still supply one group of modules. One group of modules is enough to supply the full load and charge the battery. The group of modules can be N, N+1, N+x or N+N.
