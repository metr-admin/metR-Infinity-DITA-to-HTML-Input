Annual Battery Maintenance

During the yearly maintenance, in addition to the semi-annual battery maintenance it is recommended to perform a battery discharge test.
  1. Make sure the semi-annual battery maintenance was performed. For more information see Semi-Annual Battery Maintenance.
  2. Perform one of the battery discharge tests:
    • Integrated battery test to check the condition and capacity of the batteries. For more information see How to Start the Battery Capacity Probe How to Start the Battery Capacity Test.
    • Battery discharge test according to the manufacturerâ€™s recommendation, see documentation from the battery manufacture. Record the following during the test: All cell voltages, discharge current, overall battery voltage, ambient temperature and discharge time. If wet cells are used, check the electrolyte temperature of the pilot cells.
