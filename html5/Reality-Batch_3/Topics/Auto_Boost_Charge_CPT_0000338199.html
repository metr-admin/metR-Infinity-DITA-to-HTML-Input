Auto Boost Charge

With auto boost charge enabled the system will automatically switch to auto boost if the batteries are discharged.

To enable/disable auto boost charge:

  1. Press the Hash key to enter the User stack.

  2. Use the Arrow keys to navigate to Autoboost charge.

  3. To enable: Press the Clock/number 1 key to turn on auto boost charge.

    To disable: Press the Star/number 0 key to turn off auto boost charge.

    Note: The yellow rectifier LED on the mimic diagram is active when boost charge is enabled.

  4. The system will only apply boost charge for a set time, between 0 and 99 hours (default setting is 8 hours), see Technical Data. To change this setting please contact your local Reality Service Center.

  5. Press the Back key or wait 30 seconds to exit the User stack.
