Relay Boards

There are two optional relay boards A077 and A078 with 16 relays each. The relay boards are located in the low voltage section. The input signal at X017 is sent from the controller board.

There is one relay board A077 with 16 relays. The relay board is located in the low voltage section. The input signal at X017 is sent from the controller board.

Relay board A077

The relay board A077 contains 10 12 relays for alarms from the alarm panel, and 5 4 optional alarms. The last relay is not used.

For more information about the alarms and indications see the chapter Alarm Panel.

Note: Not all alarms are used in all system types or configurations.

Relay board A078

The relay board A078 contains 10 relays for alarms from the alarm panel, and 5 status indications from the mimic diagram. The last relay indicates if the horn is active (that there are alarms that have not been confirmed).

For more information about the alarms and indications see the chapters Alarm Panel and Mimic Diagram.

Note: Not all alarms are used in all system types or configurations.
