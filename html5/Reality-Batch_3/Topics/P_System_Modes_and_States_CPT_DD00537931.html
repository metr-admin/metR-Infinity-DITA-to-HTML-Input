Operation Modes and Unit State

The systems operation mode depends on different settings and status of the system. The system will automatically detect the appropriate operation mode and automatically switch to it, depending on its configuration. It is also possible to manually switch to most operation modes.

The unit state is shown in the bottom left corner of the display. It is not necessary the same as the operation mode.

The images shows a sample of how the mimic can look for the operation mode or unit state. It is possible for some breakers to have another position than shown.
