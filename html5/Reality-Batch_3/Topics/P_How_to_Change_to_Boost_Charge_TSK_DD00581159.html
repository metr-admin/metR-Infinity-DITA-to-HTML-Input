How to Change to Boost Charge

Important: This mode is a temporary mode and should not be used for an extended period of time.
  1. Press the Switch operation icon in the Status bar.
  2. Press the Boost charge button. Confirm the change.
    Note: The current active rectifier charge mode is indicated by the green icon.
The rectifier have manually changed to boost charge. The batteries are charging at a higher DC voltage for a set period of time.
