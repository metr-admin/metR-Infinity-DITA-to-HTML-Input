How to Operate the Battery Couple Switch

CAUTION
High current flow
Only connect the battery bank when the rectifier is in operation. The DC capacitors needs to be charged by the rectifier output before the battery is connected.

The battery couple switch should only be closed if one unit needs to be isolated.

How to Close the Battery Couple Switch

To close the battery couple switch and connect the battery banks:

  1. Check that:

    1. The unit not being isolated is running in normal operation float charge.

    2. No alarms are pending on the unit not being isolated.

  2. Open the battery breaker of the unit that should be isolated.

  3. Close the battery couple switch.

  4. On the connected unit confirm any alarms by pressing the C key twice.

  5. Make sure the connected unit operates correctly and no alarms are present.

The battery banks are now connected to one unit and has the capacity of both battery banks available.

The other unit is separated from the battery and ready to be isolated.

How to Open the Battery Couple Switch

To open the battery couple switch and separate the battery banks:

  1. Check that:

    1. Both units are running in normal operation float charge.

    2. No alarms are pending on any unit, except any alarm about open battery breakers on the unit that was isolated.

  2. Open the battery couple switch.

  3. Close the open battery breaker in the isolated unit.

  4. Confirm any alarms by pressing the C key twice.

  5. Make sure the system operates correctly and no alarms are present.

The battery banks are separated and connected to each unit.
