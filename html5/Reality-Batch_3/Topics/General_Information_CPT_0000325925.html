General Information

This manual provides information about installation, operation or maintenance for Reality PxW Reality WxW Reality SDC systems.

In this manual “the system” refers to the complete single, dual or redundant system, and “the unit” refers to one of the units in a dual or redundant system. Also “the cabinet” refers to the mechanical frame of the system or unit.

In this manual “the system” refers to the complete single or redundant system, and “the unit” refers to one of the units in a redundant system. Also “the cabinet” refers to the mechanical frame of the system or unit.

The customer specific documents Single Line Diagram, Cross Reference List, Technical Data, and Circuit Diagrams are often referred to. Each system is engineered based on customer specifications and requires that you are familiar with the exact configuration of the system before installing, operating or performing maintenance on the system.

Any breaker or switch mentioned in this manual can be a switch-disconnector, fuse switch-disconnector or circuit breaker. Please see the Single Line Diagram for type used in your system.

The standard reference designators for parts are mentioned and might be different.

All images are only for illustration. The shown examples might differ from the actual system.
