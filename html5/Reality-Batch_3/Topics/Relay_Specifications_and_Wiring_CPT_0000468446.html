Relay Specifications and Wiring

DANGER
HAZARD OF ELECTRIC SHOCK
Never mix safety extra low voltage (SELV) and other voltages used for the relays on the external connection board or the relay board.

The external connection board and the relay board uses change-over (CO) relays. The table shows the required minimum voltage and current, and allowable maximum voltage and current.

Recommended Minimum Relay Rated Maximum (Resistive load) Note
6V/50mAAC 250V/6AAC 50/60Hz
6V/50mADC 250V/300mADC
6V/6ADC

The relay is in the normally open (NO) position when the terminal is energized. When the terminal is volt free the relay is in the normally closed (NC) position.
