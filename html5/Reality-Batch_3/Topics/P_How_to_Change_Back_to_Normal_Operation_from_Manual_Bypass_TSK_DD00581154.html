How to Change Back to Normal Operation from Manual Bypass

  1. Check that the BreakersPB_xx005 is closed.
  2. Close the BreakersPB_xx051 to energize the internal supply.
  3. The Display starts and after a few moments and shows the Home screen.
    Note: If active, turn off the audible buzzer by pressing the Mute icon in the upper right corner of the Display. The Mute icon is only visible if the audible buzzer is active.
  4. Close the BreakersPB_xx001 to turn on the power supply to the rectifier.
  5. Press the Switch operation icon in the Status bar.
  6. Press the Switch system on button. Confirm the change.
  7. Check that the DC voltage is in range. If the DC voltage is within tolerance, close the BreakersPB_xx004.
  8. Close the BreakersPB_xx052 to connect the inverter output to the system output.
  9. Open the BreakersPB_xx005 to disconnect the bypass mains from the system output.
  10. Press the Switch operation icon in the Status bar.
  11. Press the Normal operation button. Confirm the change.
    Note: The current active inverter operation mode is indicated by the green icon.
  12. Press the Home icon and check that the power flow indicates normal operation and that the Unit state is Online mode.
  13. Check that there are no active warnings or alarms on the Display.
The system have switched to normal operation and the load is supplied from the inverter.
