Push In Terminal Type Size ≥ 35 mm2 (AWG 2)

Insert Cables

  1. Make sure the orange rotating part (C) is in the open position (away from the center of the terminal).

  2. Insert the cable in (E) and keep the cable in place.

  3. Insert a flat screwdriver of the size 1 x 5.5 mm (0.03 x 0.21 in) in the indent (D).

  4. Use the screwdriver as a lever and rotate (C) to the closed position (towards the center of the terminal), until it snaps into place.

  5. Remove the screwdriver and make sure the cable is securely fastened.

Remove Cables

  1. Insert a flat screwdriver of the size 1 x 5.5 mm (0.03 x 0.21 in) in the indent (D).

  2. Use the screwdriver as a lever and rotate (C) to the open position (away from the center of the terminal), until it snaps into place.

  3. Remove the cable from (E).

  4. Remove the screwdriver from (C).
