Remove the Rain Roof

  1. Detach the rain roof earthing cable from the cabinet roof.
  2. Unscrew and remove the screws (A) in the roof.
  3. Lift off and remove the roof (B).
  4. Unscrew and remove the distant bolts (C) and washer (D).
    Note: The same washers (D) are used for the lifting eye bolts.
