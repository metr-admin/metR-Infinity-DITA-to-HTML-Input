LUFP7 Gateway

Notice
INCORRECT Power SUPPLY Orientation
The polarity of the power supply must be correctly oriented.
  • The negative terminal of the DC voltage supply for the LUFP7 must be connected to the negative terminal of the DC supply.
  • The positive terminal of the DC voltage supply for the LUFP7 must be connected to the positive terminal of the DC supply.
