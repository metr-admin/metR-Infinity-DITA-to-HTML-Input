Assembly of Multiple Transport Units

The transport units must be at the installation location before starting the assembly. After transport units have been attached, they can no longer be moved unless separated.

When attaching two transport units a total of ten fastening sets are needed. For the frame six fastening sets are needed , three at the front (A) and three at the back (B) and for the socket four fastening sets, two at the front (C) and two at the back (D).

Note: The shown attachment locations for the frame, (A) and (B), are only approximate and will depend on where the traverses are placed in the system.
