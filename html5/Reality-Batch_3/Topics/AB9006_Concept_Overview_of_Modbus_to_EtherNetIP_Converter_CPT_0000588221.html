Concept Overview of Modbus to EtherNet/IP Converter

To convert the alarm signals sent from the system to EtherNet/IP a AB9006 communication gateway is used. The AB9006 gateway converts the Modbus TCP/IP signal sent from the network management card (NMC).

The NMC card is required for using the AB9006 communication gateway. The connection between the NMC and the AB9006 uses a straight Ethernet cable (RJ45 connection).

A connection can then be made to the AB9006 gateway with a straight Ethernet cable (RJ45 connection) to access the EtherNet/IP signals.
