Storage Conditions

General storage conditions:

  • Storage temperature between +15°C (59°F) and +40°C (104°F).

  • 30-70% relative humidity.

  • No direct sunlight.

  • No direct storage on the floor, alongside walls, in front of radiators or other heat sources.

  • No conductive dust allowed (As per IEC 721-3-3).

Storage Inspection Plan

During long time storage the parts and packing needs to be inspected regularly. It is recommended to inspect the packing every 6 month and the part every year.

Note: If the packing is opened to visually inspect the part, the packing must be closed to its original received state before continued storage.
The packing inspection should cover:

  • Visual inspection of the packing.

  • Check that the storage condition are followed.

  • Check that the ESD protection or any extra required protection is used.

  • If necessary, replace any desiccant or drying agent.

Note: If the packing inspection reveals any irregularities or damage to the packing immediate action is required to determine if it continued long-term storage is possible. Detailed inspection of the part is required, and remedies to the storage condition or packing might be needed.
The part inspection should cover:

  • Visual inspection of the part for any signs of aging.

  • If necessary, perform any required refurbishment.
