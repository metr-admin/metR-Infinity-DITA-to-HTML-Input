Storage

DANGER
HAZARD OF ELECTRIC SHOCK, EXPLOSION, OR ARC FLASH
Always repack cabinets to their original delivered packing condition. Moisture inside cabinets can create hazardous short circuits.
Notice
Damaged Batteries
Never store batteries more than 6 months. If the batteries are de-energized for a long period of time, the batteries must be charged for 24 hours at least once every month.
Important: If vacuum sealed aluminium foil is used and the vacuum is lost and can’t be resealed, the cabinet is no longer protected from moisture.

If systems or batteries needs to be stored a short time after delivery but before installation, the systems should be stored in the transportation crate with the sealed inner packing intact.

For more information please refer to the sections:

  • Storage of the Batteries

  • Storage of the System

Recommended Storage Environments

The recommendation are for storage in the transportation crates with the sealed inner packing.

  • Storage temperature:

    • Battery: See recommendations from the original equipment manufacturer (OEM).

    • System: -10 °C to +55 °C (+14 °F to +131 °F)1

  • Relative humidity: 0–95% Non-condensing

1 For aluminum foil vacuum sealed packing with indicator instruments the storage temperature is +5 °C to +60 °C (+41 °F to +140 °F).
