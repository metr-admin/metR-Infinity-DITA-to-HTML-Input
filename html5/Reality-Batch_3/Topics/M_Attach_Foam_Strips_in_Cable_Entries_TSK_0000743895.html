Attach Foam Strips in Bottom Gland Plates

Two foam strips (C) are needed for each cable entry slot (B).
Note: The foam strips are typically delivered in a plastic bag placed under the rain roof on top of the system or inside the cabinet.
  1. Remove the adhesive tape on the back side of the foam strip (C).
  2. Place the foam strip (C) in the cable entry slot (B) so that the tape attaches to one side of the glad plate (A).
  3. Repeat until all cable entry slots (B) are covered on both sides with foam strips (C).
