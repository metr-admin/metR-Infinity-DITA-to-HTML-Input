Lift the System and Lifting Options

Warning
Risk of Heavy Objects falling
  • Only use lifting equipment rated for the weight of the cabinet.
  • Maximum system weight that can be lifted is 1400 kg (3080 lbs).

After the lifting eye bolts have been attached, lift the cabinet to the installation location.

It is recommended to strain the lifting eye bolts perpendicular (90°) to the top of the system. The lifting eye bolts can also be strained at an angle. The minimum allowed angle is 60°.

System weight: ≤ 1400 kg (3080 lbs) ≤ 1400 kg (3080 lbs)
