Alarm Panel

The alarm panel has 23 LEDs that are used for indications and alarms.

Alarm Indication

When an alarm is activated in the system the corresponding LED is activated on the alarm panel. The status of the LED indicates what alarm is the newest and if the alarms are unconfirmed or confirmed.

LED Status

Description

Off

The alarm is not active

Fast flashing

The first (initial) unconfirmed alarm

Slow flashing

Unconfirmed alarm

Constant

Confirmed present alarm

Unconfirmed alarm will not be cleared even if the alarm is not active in the system anymore. Only confirmed alarms will be cleared when they are no longer present in the system.

Confirm/Clear Alarms

To mute the audible alarm, press the Mute key.

Press the C key one time to:

  • Confirm any unconfirmed alarms. The LED will stay on but stop flashing.

  • Clear any confirmed and unconfirmed alarms that are resolved. The LED will extinguish.

  • Mute the audible alarm (if not already muted).

Note: The fast flashing LED (the first unconfirmed alarm) will stay the same after pressing the C key only once.
Press the C key a second time to:

  • Confirm the newest unconfirmed alarm. The LED will stay on but stop flashing.

  • Clear any confirmed and unconfirmed alarms that are resolved. The LED will extinguish.
