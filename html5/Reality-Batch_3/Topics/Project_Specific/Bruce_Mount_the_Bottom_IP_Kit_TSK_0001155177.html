Mount the Bottom IP Kit

Note: The fastening sets for the bottom IP kit is delivered in kit 1 (0H-9209).

The bottom front and back grids are fastened with two sets consisting of:

Position: Parts: Quantity:
(A) Screw 1
(B) Washer 1
(C) Fastening clip 1

The procedure is the same for all types of bottom IP kits; grids, gland plates and/or filter mats.

  1. Slide in any bottom grids, gland plates and/or filter mats (D) on the rails under the transport unit.
    Note: Depends on the system configuration, the selected IP rating and terminal/cable inlet placement.
  2. Only for gland plates (Not used): Fit the screws attaching the gland plates (D) to the sockets at the locations (E).
  3. Fit the clips (C). Fit the screws (A), the washers (B) and the front grid (F) to the clips (C). Screw in and tighten the screws (A).
  4. Fit the back grid (G) in the same way as the front grid (F).
