Lifting a Transport Unit with Lifting Eye Nuts

Warning
unintended lifting of Heavy Object
  • Only use lifting equipment rated for the weight of the transport unit.
  • Remove the rain roof (for systems with an IP rating of IPx1 or higher).
  • All locations for the lifting eye nuts must be used.
  • The lifting eye nuts must be tightened so that the flange of the lifting eye nut is in contact with the roof plate (or nuts).
  • For transport units heavier than 500 kg the lifting lugs must be strained perpendicularly.

Each transport unit can be lifted with lifting equipment rated for the weight of the transport unit.
