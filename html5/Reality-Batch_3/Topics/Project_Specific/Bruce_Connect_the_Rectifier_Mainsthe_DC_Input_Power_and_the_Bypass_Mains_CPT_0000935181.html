Connect the Rectifier Mains the DC Input Power and the Bypass Mains

The incoming feeders must comply with the general recommendation of IEC or the general standards applicable for the local electrical regulations.

When connecting the rectifier mains the DC input and the bypass mains consider that:

  • All wiring must comply with all applicable local and/or national electrical codes.

  • The inputs must be protected by external fuses or MCCBs.
    Note: Minimum fuse/MCCB protection rating is specified in the Technical Data. Higher rating might be needed to comply with local and/or national electrical codes.

  • Check the voltage and frequency on rating plate.

  • Pay attention to the phase rotation.

  • Pay attention to the DC input power supply polarity.

  • The person carrying out the work must be an authorized electrician.
