Transportation of the System

Warning
unintended transportation of Heavy Object
  • Always follow the transportation instructions in the Installation Manual, do not transport the system in any other way.
  • Make sure all transportation equipment used is rated for the weight of the unit.
  • Always pay attention to the center of gravity of the unit.

Check that any screws or skids fastening the system to the pallet is removed.
