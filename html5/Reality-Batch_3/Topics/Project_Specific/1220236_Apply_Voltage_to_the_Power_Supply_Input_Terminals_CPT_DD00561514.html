Apply Voltage to the Power Supply Input Terminals

The first step is to apply the specified voltages to the terminals for the system.

  1. Check that Preparations with Power Off have been performed.

  2. Check Technical Data and Connection Diagram for the specifications of the DC input power voltage and polarity.

  3. Connect the DC input power voltage to the open inverter input switch. Keep the switch open!

  4. Measure and check with a properly rated measuring device that the DC input power voltage (L+, L- and ground) and polarity are according to specifications.

  5. Check Technical Data and Connection Diagram for the specifications of the bypass mains voltage, frequency and phase sequence.

  6. Connect the bypass mains voltage to the open EN (BYP SSW) input switch. Keep the switch open!

  7. Measure and check with a properly rated measuring device that the bypass mains voltage, frequency and phase sequence are according to specifications.
