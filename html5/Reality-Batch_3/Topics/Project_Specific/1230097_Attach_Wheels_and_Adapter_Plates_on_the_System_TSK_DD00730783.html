Attach Wheels and Adapter Plates on the System

  1. Check that all transport units are attached to each other.
  2. Lift the system to a location so that the bottom of the sockets are accessible.
  3. Attach the 4 corner adapter plates. Each corner plate is attached with 2 countersunk M12 screws (A) tightened to 65 Nm (48 lb-ft), and 2 hexagon socket head with serrated flange M12 screws (B) tightened to 140 Nm (103 lb-ft).
    Note: The 2 countersunk M12 screws (A) are on the edge of the adapter plate facing either the front or the back of the system, on the edges of the sockets.
  4. Attach the 4 middle adapter plates. Each middle plate is attached with 4 hexagon socket head with serrated flange M12 screws (B) tightened to 140 Nm (103 lb-ft).
  5. Attach the 8 wheels. Each wheel is attached on the adapter plates with 4 hexagon socket head with serrated flange M8 screws (C) tightened to 42 Nm (31 lb-ft).
  6. Check that all eight adapter plates and all eight wheels are attached. Check that all screws are tightened to the specified torque.
  7. Lift the system so that it is standing on the wheels.
The system can now be moved on wheels.
