Connect the Earth Busbars to the Gland Plates

Important: All the gland plates in the system must be connected to the earth busbars.

Connect Top Mounted Gland Plates

  1. Check that top earth busbar (D) is connected to the protective earth through the gland plate.

  2. Attach the top earth cables (H) to the top earth busbar (D) with the fastening set (E). Tighten to 16 Nm (11.8 lb-ft).

    Note: The busbar fastenings sets are typically already attached to the busbar.

  3. Attach the top earth cables (H) to each gland plate with the fastening sets (I). Tighten to 10 Nm (7.38 lb-ft).

    Note: Depending on the configuration there can be either one or two gland plates.
