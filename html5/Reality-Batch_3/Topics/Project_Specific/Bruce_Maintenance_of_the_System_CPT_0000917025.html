Maintenance of the System

DANGER
HAZARD OF ELECTRIC SHOCK, EXPLOSION, OR ARC FLASH
Before performing any maintenance inside the system:
  • Read the safety information
  • Use appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Isolate all the power supplies (including the battery) from the system and use lockout-tagout (LOTO)
  • Wait 5 minutes after isolating the system to let the DC capacitors discharge
  • Check that any removable (NC) earth link is closed
  • Check that the parts, capacitors, terminal and protective earth are volt free with a properly rated voltage sensing device
  • Check that protective covers are installed on any energized parts
Important: Always perform maintenance and preventive maintenance according to specifications to ensure reliable operation.
Note: Always record and document the performed maintenance. This is important to identify adjustments to the maintenance and replacement schedules according to the specific system and environment.

Maintenance Schedule Overview

The preventive maintenance for the system under normal conditions consists of:

  • A monthly check

  • A semi-annual check

  • An annual check

Depending on the environment and condition of the system it can be recommended to perform checks more frequently. Reality provides several service offers to make your maintenance easier, for more information see Overview of the Customer Service Offers.
