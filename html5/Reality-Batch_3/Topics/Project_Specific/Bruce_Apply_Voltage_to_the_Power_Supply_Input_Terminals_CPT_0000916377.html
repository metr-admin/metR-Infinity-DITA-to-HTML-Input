Apply Voltage to the Power Supply Input Terminals

The first step is to apply the specified voltages to the terminals for the units.

  1. Check that Preparations with Power Off have been performed.

  2. Check Technical Data and Connection Diagram for the specifications of the rectifier mains voltage, frequency and phase sequence.

  3. Check Technical Data and Connection Diagram for the specifications of the DC input power voltage and polarity.

  4. Connect the rectifier mains voltage to the open rectifier mains input breaker(s). Keep the breaker(s) open!

  5. Measure and check with a properly rated measuring device that the rectifier mains voltage, frequency and phase sequence are according to specifications.

  6. Connect the DC input power voltage to the open inverter input switches. Keep the switches open!

  7. Measure and check with a properly rated measuring device that the DC input power voltage (L+, L- and ground) and polarity are according to specifications.

  8. On the bypass:

    1. Check Technical Data and Connection Diagram for the specifications of the bypass mains voltage, frequency and phase sequence.

      Important: The same bypass mains source must be used for all units.

    2. Connect the bypass mains voltage to the open bypass mains input breaker. Keep the breaker open!

    3. Measure and check with a properly rated measuring device that the bypass mains voltage, frequency and phase sequence are according to specifications.
