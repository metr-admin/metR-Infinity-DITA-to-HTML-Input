Float Charge

In float charge operation the rectifier supplies the load and charges the battery with the float voltage setting.

The rectifier mains input feeds the phase angle controlled rectifier with a galvanically isolated transformer and converts the AC to DC. The rectifier regulation maintains the set float voltage for input voltage variations and various DC loads.
