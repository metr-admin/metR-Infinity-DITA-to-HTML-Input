Connect the Protective Earth to the Earth Busbars

Each earth busbar is labeled with a protective earth symbol.

Important: All earth busbars in the system must be connected to the protective earth.

Connect Top Earth Busbars

  1. Check that the gland plates have all the required holes drilled and that they are firmly fixed on the roof.

  2. Check that the earth cable is connected to the protective earth at the installation location.

  3. Pull the earth cable (C) through the already drilled hole in the top gland plate.

  4. Attach the earth cable (C) to the top earth busbar (D) with the fastening set (E). Tighten to 16 Nm (11.8 lb-ft).

    Note: The busbar fastenings sets are typically already attached to the busbar.
