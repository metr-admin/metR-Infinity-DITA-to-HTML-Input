Boost Charge

Boost charge mode can be entered manually through the system interface or automatically if auto boost charge is enabled. For example, boost charge can be activated automatically after a period of battery operation or a discharge test. In boost charge the rectifier output voltage is higher than in float charge.

It is possible to inhibit the change of charge mode. This inhibits both manual and automatic change of charge mode.
