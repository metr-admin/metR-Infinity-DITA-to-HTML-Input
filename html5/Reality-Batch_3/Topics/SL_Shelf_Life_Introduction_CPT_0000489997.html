Shelf Life Introduction

The long lifetime of Reality systems is possible due to proper maintenance, service and when needed spare part replacement. Depending on the system application and environment, it might be necessary to store parts for a period of time. Before storing a part it is important to consider the shelf life, storage condition and type of packing so the part can fulfil its design life.

In this document the Reality definition of shelf life and design life are defined. For applicable parts the shelf life, storage condition and packing specifications are specified.
