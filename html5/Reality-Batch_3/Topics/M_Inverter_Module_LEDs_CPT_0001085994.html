Inverter Module LEDs

On the front of the inverter modules there are two LEDs. One red (A) and one green (B). The LEDs indicate the status of the individual module.

Status LED Description LED Visualization
Not in operation
  • Red: Off
  • Green: Off
Initialization
  • Red: Flashing
  • Green: Flashing
Operation, load <90% of maximum power
  • Red: Off
  • Green: On
Operation but close to load limit, load >90% of maximum power
  • Red: Off
  • Green: Flashing
Overloaded, load >90% of maximum power
  • Red: On
  • Green: Flashing
Module inoperable
  • Red: On
  • Green: Off
