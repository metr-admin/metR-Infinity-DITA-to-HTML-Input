IGBT Rectifier

The rectifier is a bi-directional IGBT rectifier. The rectifier converts the 3 phase alternating current (AC) input to a direct current (DC). A capacitor filter smoothens the DC output that supplies the inverter and charges the battery. The IGBT rectifier works with other components to achieve a very low inrush current.

It is also possible for the IGBT rectifier to act as an inverter and convert DC from the internal bus to AC that is feed to the mains. This can be used during battery discharge tests, for quick DC stabilization and for future energy management applications.
