Remove the Rain Roof

If the transport unit has a rain roof (IPx1 or higher) it must be removed so that the lifting eye nuts can be mounted.
Note: Before removing the rain roof it is recommended to mark the location for where the cable entries should be drilled.
  1. Unscrew and remove all screws (A) that attach the rain roof to the transport unit.
  2. Carefully lift off all the sections of the rain roof (B).
  3. Unscrew and remove the pins (C) used for the rain roof.
  4. Now the threaded M12 bolt (D) for the lifting eye nuts can be accessed.
