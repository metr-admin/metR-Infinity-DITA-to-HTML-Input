Replacement Schedule

As part of the standard replacement schedule the following parts need to be replaced.

Table 1. Standard Replacement Parts
Part Name Replacement Cycle Notes
Cabinet transformer fans Every 4-6 years
Input AC capacitor(s) Every 8-10 years
Output AC capacitor(s) Every 8-10 years
Rectifier capacitor sub-assembly Every 8-10 years Part of the complete power module.
Inverter capacitor sub-assembly Every 8-10 years Part of the complete power module.
Power supply units (PSU) Every 8-10 years
Battery See documentation from the battery manufacturer

As part of an extended replacement schedule it is recommended to replace the following parts, if applicable.

Table 2. Extended Replacement Parts
Part Name Replacement Cycle Notes
Display Every 8-10 years
Monitoring controller Every 8-10 years
Monitoring controller extension board Every 8-10 years Optional
Fan monitoring card Every 8-10 years
Measuring board Every 8-10 years
Power distribution board Every 8-10 years
Rectifier power module Every 8-10 years If the complete power module is replaced, this is done instead of replacing the capacitor assembly.
Inverter power module Every 8-10 years If the complete power module is replaced, this is done instead of replacing the capacitor assembly.
