Installation Supervision and Commissioning

Service engineer(s) supervise the installation process and performs the commissioning of the systems on site.

The supervision includes the follow inspections:

  • Location and environment for the system and battery

  • System and battery wiring connections

  • Internal power and communication components

The commissioning process also includes:

  • Check and record measurements for the system input, system output and voltage for each individual cell

  • A battery bank discharge test

  • If necessary, additional calibrations and adjustments

Preventive Maintenance

  • Yearly preventive maintenance

  • 4-6 years preventive maintenance, minor material replacement

  • 8-10 years preventive maintenance, major material replacement

Corrective Maintenance

Our service engineers are available to assist on-site in the event that technical repairs or part replacement services are needed.

Spare Parts

  • Spare parts used during commissioning

  • Spare parts used during preventive maintenance

  • Strategical and critical spare parts to have on stock, with a spare parts and interchangeability record (SPIR)

Updates, Upgrades and Modifications

Software and firmware updates to the latest revision with the latest functions.

During a system’s lifespan customer needs might change and modifications might be necessary. Optional features may be added to existing systems for example a network management card (NMC) or a battery management system (BMS).

Digital Services

Monitor and manage critical assets on site or remotely with high cyber security can increase efficiency. It can help to efficiently operate selected systems and reduce operation costs with flexible service plans.

Battery Replacement

The lifetime of batteries varies between 4 and 20 years depending on the type of battery, environmental conditions and maintenance.

Even with proper maintenance batteries will have to be replaced at some time. Reality provides service offers for preventive maintenance and replacement of batteries.

Retrofit or Replace

Retrofit: Replace an existing system with the same design and dimensions as the existing system.

Replace: Replace an existing system with a new design meeting the latest industry requirement.

Site Audit

Reality system have a designed lifetime of 20 years, during this time your site might grow or change. Reality can assist with a detailed on-site check to:

  • Prepare for future service recommendations

  • Discuss retrofit or replacement for systems nearing expected end of life

  • Organize installed base

  • Review of the site environment for external factors that can affect the system
