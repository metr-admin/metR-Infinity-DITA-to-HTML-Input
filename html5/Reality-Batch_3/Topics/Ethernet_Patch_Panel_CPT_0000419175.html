Ethernet Patch Panel FL-PP-RJ45-SC

The Ethernet patch panel is the interface between the LUFP7 gateway and the secondary Modbus devices. It is used to convert the Modbus RTU signal from the secondary devices to a RJ45 signal that is sent to the gateway.

For detailed information about the Ethernet patch panel please refer to the Reality page and data sheet https://www.phoenixcontact.com/online/portal/us?uri=pxc-oc-itemdetail:pid=2901643&library=usen&tab=1
