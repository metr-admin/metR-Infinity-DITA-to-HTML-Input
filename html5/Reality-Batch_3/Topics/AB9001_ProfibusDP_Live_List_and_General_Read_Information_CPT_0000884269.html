Live List and General Read Information

In the live list one server is constantly monitors the status of the transactions for the analog and digital values. Only one of the eight servers and only two bits are used. If the connection to the analog and digital readings are ok byte 8 will read 00000011.

The digital and analog lists both have a scan time of 250 ms and a timeout of 5 s. They are also configured as freeze data to server. Meaning the lists will show the latest value it received incase there is a communication interruption.
