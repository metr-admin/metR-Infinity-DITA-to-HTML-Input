Attach the G.BMS Modules

To attach the G.BMS modules on the batteries:

  1. Remove the adhesive film from the fastening strips. Each module has two strips.
  2. Position the module over the correct placement.
  3. Press down and hold the module under moderate pressure for 5-10 seconds.
  4. Check that the module is securely fixed on the battery.
  5. Connect the measuring cable plug to the socket at the bottom of the module.
    Note: The measuring cable is equipped with a mechanical protection against accidental polarity reversal.
