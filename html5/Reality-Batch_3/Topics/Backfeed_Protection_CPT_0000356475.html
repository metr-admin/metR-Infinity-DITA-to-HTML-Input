Backfeed Protection

All systems must be fitted with either:

  • Internal backfeed protection

  • External backfeed protection

The internal backfeed protection is an option that can be selected.
Note: If selected, the internal backfeed protection is shown in the Single Line Diagram for the system.

Backfeed protection helps to prevent the occurrence of any hazardous voltages at the AC supply terminals in the case of an AC mains failure due to unexpected backfeed from the inverter operating in stored energy mode.
