Attach the Frames

Note: The fastening sets for the frames are delivered in kit 3 (0H-9208).

The frames are fastened with six sets consisting of:

Position: Parts: Quantity: Recommended Torque:
(E) M8x20 hexagon bolt 2 16 Nm (11.8 lb-ft)
(F) Hexagon standoff 30 mm with M8 internal thread 1
(G) M8/8.2 tooth lock washers 2
  1. Place the hexagon standoff (F) between the frames of the two unit at a large hole (H) in one of the locations at (A) or (B).
  2. Screw in the hexagon bolt (E) with the tooth lock washers (G) into the hexagon standoff (F).
  3. Make sure the other unit is in place and correctly mounted to the floor.
    Note: After the transport units are attached, they need to be separated again if they need to be moved/lifted/transported.
  4. Screw in the second hexagon bolt (E) with the second tooth lock washers (G).
  5. Repeat the previous steps for the other five locations in the front (A) and back (B).
  6. Tighten all the hexagon bolts (E) to the recommended torque of 16 Nm (11.8 lb-ft).
