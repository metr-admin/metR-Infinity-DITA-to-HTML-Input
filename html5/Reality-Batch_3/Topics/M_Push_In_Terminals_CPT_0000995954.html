Push In Terminals

Notice
damage to the Terminal
Always use screwdrivers of an appropriate size for the push in terminal. A too large or too small screwdriver can damage the terminal.
Only use flat screwdrivers and always apply pressure carefully.
Terminal Type Size Flat Screwdriver Size Note
≤ 4 mm2 (AWG 12) 0.6 x 3.5 mm (0.02 x 0.13 in) A flat screwdriver is always needed to remove the cable. For terminals larger than 35 mm2 (AWG 2) a screwdriver is also needed to insert the cable.
≥ 6 mm2 (AWG 10) 1 x 5.5 mm (0.03 x 0.21 in)
For information about how to connect and remove cables for the terminals refer to the sections:

  • Push In Terminal Type Size ≤ 16 mm2 (AWG 6)

  • Push In Terminal Type Size ≥ 35 mm2 (AWG 2)
