Digital Values

The digital values are displayed for each byte as a decimal value. Convert the decimal value to binary and it will indicate what alarm bits are active in that byte.

For example if the decimal value 96 is shown for byte 9 (the first byte in the digital value list) this corresponds to the binary value of 01100000. This means that the alarms for bit 6 and 7 in byte 9 are active.
