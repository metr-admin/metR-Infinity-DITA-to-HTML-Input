Recommended Protective Earth Cable Dimensions

For the protective earth (PE) cables sizes it is recommended to follow the table below based on IEC 62477-1:2012. The table only lists recommended minimum size. Always follow applicable local and/or national electrical codes.

The size of the protective earth cable depends on the size of the used power cables. For the recommended minimum size of the power cables, see the chapter Recommended Power Cable Dimensions.

Important: The calculated values assume the PE cable is made of the same material as the power cable. If the material is different, the PE cross-section area needs to be determined so that the conductance is equivalent to the result from the table.
Note: The calculated values are only for cross-sectional areas in mm2. If the power cable size is specified in AWG or kcmil convert to mm2 and use the table, then convert back to AWG or kcmil.
