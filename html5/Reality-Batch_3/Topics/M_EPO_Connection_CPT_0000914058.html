EPO Connection

The emergency power off (EPO) is wired as normally open (NO) to the dry contacts (A) and (B) on the controller.

  • (A) Digital input 8.
  • (B) The internal 24 V DC power supply.

If the circuit is closed the EPO signal is sent to the controller. The EPO signal trips the output breaker and the load is no longer supplied by the system.
