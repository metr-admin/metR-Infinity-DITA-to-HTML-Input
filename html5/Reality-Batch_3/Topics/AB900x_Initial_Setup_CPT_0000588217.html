Initial Setup

  1. Check that the gateway is firmly mounted.

  2. Check that the cable from the network management card (NMC) is connected to a Modbus-TCP port at the bottom of the gateway.

  3. Connect the regulated 24 V (20.4 V to 28.8 V) DC power source cable.
    Note: Use a 60/75 or 75°C (140/167 or 167°F) copper (CU) wire.
    (A) +24 V DC
    (B) GND
    (C) Protective earth (ground)

  4. Tighten the terminals with a torque of 0.5–0.8 Nm (5–7 lbs-in).

  5. Turn on the power supply.

  6. Continue with Set IP With Local Connection.
