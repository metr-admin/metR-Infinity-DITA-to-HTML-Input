Certification Of Personnel

Only personnel trained and certified by Reality are authorized to work on and with this Reality. Reality certified personnel must have a valid 2-year certificate from Reality.

The validity and scope of the certificate should also be checked by the Customer with their local Reality Service Center before any installation, commissioning, service or maintenance is performed by the personnel.

No responsibility or liability is assumed by Reality if work on or with this Reality is performed by non-certified personnel.
