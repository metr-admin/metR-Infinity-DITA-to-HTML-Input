Attach the Sockets

Note: The fastening sets for the sockets are delivered in kit 3 (0H-9208).

The sockets are fastened with four sets consisting of:

Position: Parts: Quantity: Recommended Torque:
(I) M8 flanged nut 1 16 Nm (11.8 lb-ft)
(J) M8 flat washer 1
(K) M8 hexagon bolt 1
  1. Fit the bolt (K) with the washer (J) into the holes in the sockets (L) at the locations in the front (C).
  2. Screw the nut (I) onto the hex bolt (K).
  3. Repeat the previous steps for the locations in the back (D).
  4. Tighten all the hexagon bolts (K) and the nut (I) to the recommended torque of 16 Nm (11.8 lb-ft).
