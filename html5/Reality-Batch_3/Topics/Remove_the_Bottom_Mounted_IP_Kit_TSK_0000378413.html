Remove the Bottom Mounted IP Kit

The procedure for removing the bottom mounted IP kit is the same for all types of IP ratings.

  1. Unscrew the screws (A) from the clips (C). Remove the screws (A), the washers (B), the clips (C) and the front grid (D) from the transport units.
  2. Remove the back grid (E) in the same way as the front grid (D).
  3. Only for gland plates: Remove the screws attaching the gland plates (F) to the sockets at the locations (G).
  4. Slide out and remove any bottom grids, filter mats and/or gland plates (F) from under the transport unit.
    Note: Depends on the system configuration, the selected IP rating and terminal/cable inlet placement. See the general arrangement drawing for more information.
