System Space Requirements

The system has recommended and minimum clearances from the back (A) and sides (B) to the walls, and from the top (C) to the roof. The recommended clearances make it easier to install and perform maintenance on the system. The minimum clearances are minimum requirement for airflow and to fasten the system to the floor. See the table for values.

Important: The recommended clearances are only general guidelines. Always consult with and follow the local safety codes and standards for additional requirements on clearances in your local and/or national area.
