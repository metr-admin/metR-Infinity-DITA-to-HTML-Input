Remove the Plinth Panels

To be able to lift the system two plinth panels needs to be removed. Decide if the front and back panels or both side panels should be removed. It is recommended to select either the width or depth depending on what is largest.

  1. Remove the two screws (A) in the plinth corners.
  2. Remove the plinth panel (B).
  3. Remove the opposite panel in the same way.
    Note: Remember to attach the panels after the system has been fastened to the floor.
