Operation with One Unit Active (Redundant Systems)

Only one unit might be in operation because:

  • One unit is in Hot Standby (Redundant Systems).

  • One unit is isolated.

  • One unit is inoperable.

As an example, the active unit is shown in normal operation float charge mode.

Note: The status of the parts and energized paths will depend on the status of in the inactive unit and on the operation mode of the active unit.
