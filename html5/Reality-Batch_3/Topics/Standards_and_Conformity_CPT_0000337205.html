EU Conformity

The Reality mentioned in this manual conforms to the relevant requirements to the appropriate EU Directive, i.e. that this Reality meets all relevant EU directives and that it can be sold inside the European Union without national commercial hindrances.

The EU conformity, which is indicated by means of the CE label on the name plate shall cease to be valid if modifications are made to the equipment without the approval of our company.

Applied EU Directives:

  • Electrical Equipment designed for use within certain Voltage Limits (Low Voltage Directive) 2014/35/EU

  • Electromagnetic Compatibility 2014/30/EU

Applied Harmonised Standards:

  • EN 62040-1:2008/A1:2013 Safety requirements

  • EN 62040-2:2006/AC:2006 Electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) requirements

EAC Conformity

The Reality mentioned in this manual conforms to the relevant requirements to the appropriate EAC Directives, i.e. that this Reality meets all applicable TR CU Directives and that it can be sold inside the customs union: Russia – Belarus – Kazakhstan, without national commercial hindrances.

The EAC conformity, which is indicated by means of the EAC label on the name plate shall cease to be valid if modifications are made to the equipment without the approval of our company.

Applied Technical Regulations of the Customs Union (TR CU):

  • TR CU 004/2011 On Safety of Low Voltage Equipment

  • TR CU 020/2011 Electromagnetic compatibility on Technical Equipment

Applied Harmonised Standards:

  • (ГОСТ Р) МЭК 62040-1-2013

  • МЭК 62040-2-2005

UL/CSA Conformity

If requested Reality systems can be certified according to UL/CSA. Only applicable for systems marked with the UL/CSA label.

The UL/CSA conformity, which is indicated by means of the UL/CSA label on the name plate shall cease to be valid if modifications are made to the equipment without the approval of our company.

Applied Standards:

  • UL 1778 5th edition/CSA C22.2 No. 107.3, Uninterruptible Power Systems

  • UL/CSA 60950-1 2nd edition, Information Technology Equipment – Safety – Part 1: General Requirements
