Fasten the Cabinet to the Floor

Each system has a plinth at the bottom of the cabinet with the height of 100 mm. The plinth needs to be fastened to the floor at the installation site.

For dimensions and details of the plinth, see the Drawings.

Important: Always fasten the cabinet to the floor before connecting any cables.

  • The locations (A) are accessed from the back of the cabinet between the plinths with the back plinth plate cover removed.

  • The locations (B) are accessed from the front of the cabinet between the plinths with the front plinth plate cover removed.
