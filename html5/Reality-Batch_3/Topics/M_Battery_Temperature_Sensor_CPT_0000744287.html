Battery Temperature Sensor

The battery temperature sensor can either be connected at the controller or in the terminal section.

In systems with internal batteries the temperature sensor is already installed in the factory and connected to the controller.

For external batteries the temperature sensor must be connected at the terminal section to a terminal, for the reference designator and more details see the Drawings.

Note: After the batteries are installed the position of the temperature sensor should be checked. It is recommended to place the temperature sensor close to a battery cell in the middle of the battery bank.
