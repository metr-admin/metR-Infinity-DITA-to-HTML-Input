Packing Specifications

CAUTION
Electrostatic Discharge
Only open, repack or handle electrostatic sensitive parts at static-free workstations.
The type of packing bag used mainly depends on the required ESD protection:

  • Static shielding bag (high ESD protection).

  • Black conductive bag (medium ESD protection).

  • Pink antistatic bag (only for preventing ESD).

Note: For certain parts packing bags with additional qualities may be used.
