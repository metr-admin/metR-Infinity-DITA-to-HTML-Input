Using Power Invoice Generation: Salesforce Revenue Cloud

Starting on October 4th, completing the installation of the latest Composer package (8.263) and setting your Composer edition to “Salesforce Revenue Cloud” you can benefit from Power Invoice Generation: Salesforce Revenue Cloud, allowing you to customize and export invoices, quotes, and receipts, providing a free and powerful way to generate documents.
