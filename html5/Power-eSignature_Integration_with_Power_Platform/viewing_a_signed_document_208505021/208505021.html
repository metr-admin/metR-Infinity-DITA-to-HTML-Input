Viewing a Signed Document with Power Sign

Once all signatories have signed the document, Power Sign sends you an email that signing is completed. The email notification contains a link to open the signed document. The contract's status changes to fully signed.

Parent topic: Working with Power Sign

To view a signed document

  1. Open the contract record.
  2. Navigate to the Documents links.
  3. Select the document with a "_signed" suffix.

    or

    Open the email notification and click DOWNLOAD COMPLETED DOCUMENTS.
