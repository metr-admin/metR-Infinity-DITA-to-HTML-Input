Creating Apps and Keys in DocuSign

In order to integrate DocuSign with the Power eSignature service, you need to create an app and an integration key as follows:

  1. Log in to your DocuSign account as an administrator.
  2. Go to Settings > Integrations > Apps and Keys.
  3. Click Add App and Integration Key.
  4. Enter the app name, and click Create App button.
  5. Copy the Integration Key to a notepad from General Info section.
  6. Under the Authentication section, click Add Secret Key button. Copy the secret key to a notepad.
  7. Under the Additional Settings section, click Add URI. Add URI: {Power Platform Instance Url}/Poweradmin/integrations/details/DocuSign
  8. Click Save.
    Note:

    You need integration key, secret key, and redirect URL values to integrate the Power eSignature service with DocuSign.
