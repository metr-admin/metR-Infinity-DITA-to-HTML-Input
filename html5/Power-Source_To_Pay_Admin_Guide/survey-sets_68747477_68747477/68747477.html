Survey Sets

Survey Sets are the surveys that are sent out to the suppliers any time after they have onboarded. Survey Sets enable the buyers to send out compliance surveys to multiple suppliers, simultaneously. The external users populate the surveys and the compliance is decided as per the responses. You can send selected surveys to thousands of suppliers simultaneously. You can set deadlines and validity to a survey as well.

In order to send out the surveys to your suppliers, you must perform the following steps in the mentioned order.

  • Creating a Survey Set
  • Publishing the Survey Set Created

After you have published the survey sets, you can manage the responses from the Responses tab in Survey Set screen. The Responses tab is visible only after you publish the survey.

  • Viewing the responses of survey set
  • Changing contact and resending survey
Tip:

Use Case

In our example, Sam can send out surveys any time. Survey Sets can be utilized to send surveys to the entire groups. Sam sends out an additional survey for the Microphone Suppliers supplier group.

Creating a survey set

  1. Click the Survey Sets tab.
  2. Enter the Survey Set Name.
  3. Complete the following fields.

    Field Name

    Description

    Survey Set Name

    Enter a descriptive Survey Set Name.

    Description

    Enter a description of survey set.

    Survey Deadline

    Enter a Survey Deadline for suppliers.

    Period From

    Enter a start date of the survey set.

    Period To

    Enter an end date of the survey set.
  4. Expand the Additional Info section and enter a Detailed Description of your survey set.
  5. Under Survey Details section, click the Suppliers tab.
  6. Select a supplier group from Supplier picklist menu. A list of supplier groups present in the system are displayed.
  7. You can select a relevant contact from the Contact Name picklist menu for each supplier.
  8. Click + Add Recipients to add more suppliers.
    1. In the Suppliers List window, search and select the suppliers you want to add.
    2. For the selected supplier, select a contact from the Contact Name picklist menu for each supplier.
    3. Click Add.
    Note:

    Suppliers can be added to a Survey Set even after the survey set has been published. On adding a new supplier, an email is sent to the new supplier contact with the link to the survey.

  9. Click Add.
  10. Click the Surveys tab.
  11. Enter a keyword to search a survey. Press Enter key after you find a relevant survey.
  12. Click the icon to upload an attachment. -or- To delete a survey that you have added, click the icon.
  13. In the Attachment dialog box, click Upload.
  14. Select the file that you want to upload and click Open. The document you upload will be accessible by all your suppliers. Suppliers can not delete the documents that you upload.
    Note:

    Note

    The attachments that you or your suppliers upload are added to Notes & Attachments related list of the respective Account or Survey.

  15. Click Done.
  16. Click Save.

Publishing a survey set

You must have an existing survey set.

  1. Click the Survey Sets tab.
  2. Select the survey that you want to publish.
  3. Click Publish to publish the survey set.

    -or-

    Click Edit to edit the survey set.

    -or-

    Click More and then click Clone to clone this survey set.

    -or-

    Click More and then select Cancel Survey Set to cancel this survey set.

  4. Click Yes to Publish the Survey Set.
    Note:

    Note

    After you publish the survey, Responses tab is visible. You can access the responses of the Supplier Surveys from this tab.

Viewing the responses of a survey set

You must publish the survey before you view the responses.

  1. Click the Survey Sets tab.
  2. Select the survey set for which you want to view the responses.
  3. Click the Responses tab.
    Note:

    Note

    Responses tab is visible only if you have published the survey set.

  4. To filter the responses based on a Supplier, from the Supplier picklist menu and select the supplier for whom you want to view the responses.

    -or-

    To filter the responses based on a Survey, from the Survey picklist menu and select the survey for which you want to view the responses.

  5. To view the information of supplier and the responses of this survey, click View for the supplier that you want to view the responses of.
  6. To upload an attachment, click Upload.
  7. Select the file that you want to upload and click Open. The document you upload will be accessible by all your suppliers. Suppliers can not delete the documents that you upload.
    Note:

    Note

    The attachments that you or your suppliers upload are added to Notes & Attachments related list of the respective Account or Survey.

  8. You can populate the responses on behalf of your suppliers as well. Populate responses in the survey and click Next.
  9. Click Submit after you have finished filling the survey.
  10. To Download the entire survey set response, click Download. A file in .xls format is downloaded.
  11. Click the Back Arrow to go back to Surveys list page.

Changing supplier contact and resending survey

You can change the supplier contact and resend the survey even after a survey has been published. For changing supplier contact:

  1. Click the Survey Sets tab.
  2. Select the survey set for which you want to view the responses and click Edit.
  3. Click the Suppliers tab.
  4. Select the new contact from the Contact Name picklist, corresponding to the supplier whose contact you want to change.
  5. Click Resend from the response column of the supplier list.
  6. Click Save.

The new contact will get an email with the link to the survey, whereas the old contact will get an email informing about the cancellation of survey.
