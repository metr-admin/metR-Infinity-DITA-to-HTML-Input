Managing the Responses

Responses are the information provided by the suppliers or stakeholders. The responses that you receive from the suppliers for your sourcing event are saved under Responses tab. You can then evaluate the suppliers based on the responses you received.

There are three types of responses.

  • Managing RFI Responses , Information provided by the supplier in response to RFI sent by the buyer.
  • Managing RFQ Responses , Information provided by the supplier in response to RFQ sent by the buyer.
  • Managing the Surveys , Information provided by the stakeholder in response to Survey sent by the buyer.
  • Managing RFI Responses
    RFI Responses are the responses sent by the suppliers to the buyers in response to the RFI sent by the buyer. RFI Responses allows the buyer to view the RFI responses submitted by the suppliers. RFI can contain all the questions that you would like to get information for, from suppliers. RFI includes questions related to general information, financial information, safety information, quality information, and much more.
