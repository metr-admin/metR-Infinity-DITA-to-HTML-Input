Creating Compliance Type

A Compliance Type is a parameter to check whether a supplier is compliant to participate in a sourcing event. You can determine the compliance of a supplier by adding various surveys to which the supplier will respond to. Once you assign surveys to a compliance type, and assign a compliance type to a supplier group. After assigning a supplier group to a supplier, the supplier receives the survey associated to compliance type.

To add a compliance type

  1. On the SRM Setup Console tab, click Compliance Type.

    -or-

    Click the Compliance Type tab.

  2. Click New.
  3. Enter a descriptive Name.
  4. Enter a Description.
  5. Click Add Surveys.
  6. Type in the name of a survey that you want to add in the Surveys type-ahead box and press the Enter key to add a relevant survey.
  7. Click Save.
