Evaluating the Suppliers

The Buyer has to create a Scorecard and the suppliers are rated on the basis of the criteria set in a scorecard. The Evaluation can be reopened if the Award has not been completed. The Survey Score will be visible only after you click Complete Survey button.

By evaluating the suppliers, you can

  • Score the supplier responses using a scorecard.
  • Collect Stakeholder Surveys for each supplier.
  • Compare supplier responses.
  • Identify suppliers to be eliminated from the selection process.
  • Identify suppliers to be selected for the next step of the sourcing event.
Tip:

Use Case

Sam has to do a final evaluation of whom to award the deal. He evaluates the suppliers on the basis of RFI Score, RFQ Score, and Survey Score.

Parent topic: Strategic Sourcing User Roles

To start evaluation

  1. Click Sourcing Events tab.
  2. Select the appropriate sourcing event and click Edit.
  3. Click the Evaluation tab.
  4. The results are displayed in the Evaluation section.
