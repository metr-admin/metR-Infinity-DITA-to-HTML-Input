Adding Evaluation Weightage

The Buyer has to set an Evaluation Weightage on the basis of which the scores are calculated. Evaluation Weightage is utilised in calculating the weighted score of the entire sourcing event.

The Buyer has to enter the weight in percentage. Evaluation weightage provides the list of the three weights used for calculating the total average score for a supplier. The suppliers are scored depending on the following fields.

  • RFI
  • RFQ
  • Survey

To add evaluation weightage

  1. Click the Sourcing Events tab.
  2. Select the appropriate sourcing event and clickEdit.
  3. Click the Preparations tab and click the Evaluation Weightage tab.
  4. Enter appropriate weight for each category.
    Note:
    Ensure that the sum of the Evaluation Weightage of RFI, RFQ, and Survey is equal to 100%.
  5. Click Save.
  6. Click Complete Preparation.
    Note:

    Note

    Skip this step if you have selected Auto Mode as your Event Mode Type in your sourcing event type. For Auto Mode, the phase progression is carried out automatically, based on the date and time mentioned in Milestones.

    Per

    Note:

    Note

    A Phase Progression pop-up is displayed for the confirmation of completing the phase. Click Yes to proceed to next phase. Click No to continue working in the same phase.

  7. Click Publish Event.
    Note:

    Note

    The sourcing event will be visible to suppliers only after you click Publish Event. You must add Suppliers and Milestones before you publish the event.
