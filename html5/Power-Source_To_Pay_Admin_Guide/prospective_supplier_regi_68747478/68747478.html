Prospective Supplier Registration

Prospective Supplier Registration enables the salesforce internal users or the suppliers to create a new request for the supplier registration. The Prospective Supplier Request form collects the business information, tax related information, products or services, service locations, certifications and financial information. Suppliers can self-register themselves as well. Onboarding surveys are also attached with the form to collect more details.

A supplier can be registered in the system by two methods.

  • Prospective Supplier Requests
  • Supplier Self-Registration
