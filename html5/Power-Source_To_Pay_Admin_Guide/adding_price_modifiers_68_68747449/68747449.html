Adding Price Modifiers

Price Modifiers are the conditions that you can include in RFQ to see the difference in total pricing of the supplier in presence of constraints or flexibility.

Price Modifiers work at the RFQ Response level and affects the total pricing. The Price Modifier adjustment can be an incentive or a surcharge.

Tip:

Use Case

Sam wants to know the difference between the rates of a standard delivery and 1- Day delivery. So, he will add them as price modifiers.

To add a price modifier

  1. Click the Sourcing Events tab.
  2. Select the appropriate sourcing event and click Edit.
  3. Click the Preparation tab and click the Price Modifiers tab.
  4. Enter the name of the price modifier in the Condition text box.
  5. Click Add.
  6. Click Save to save the sourcing event.
