Adding the Stakeholders

Stakeholders, the internal members of the organization who organize the sourcing event, participate in the survey to rate the shortlisted suppliers on different performance indicators. The Stakeholders provide their opinion on the suppliers by Stakeholder Survey. Stakeholders must evaluate the suppliers on a neutral basis.

The stakeholder is notified of the updates of the events and receives notifications related to the sourcing event. He is allowed to see the details of a sourcing event and can respond to a survey for each supplier response.

To add a stakeholder

  1. Click the Sourcing Events tab.
  2. Select the sourcing event you want to edit and click Edit.
  3. Click the Preparation tab and click the Stakeholders tab.
  4. Click Add.
  5. Enter relevant Search Criteria details and click Search.
  6. Select relevant stakeholders from the Search Results section.
    Note:

    Note

    The selected stakeholders are displayed in the Selected Results section.

  7. ClickSave Selection.
    Note:

    Caution

    Ensure that the selected Stakeholders are added to the Stakeholders section on the Sourcing Events page.
  8. From Score Categories column, assign relevant score category to each stakeholder for scoring. If you want your stakeholder to evaluate each category, select All.
  9. Click Save.
