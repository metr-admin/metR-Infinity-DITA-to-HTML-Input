Preparing for the Sourcing Event

You have to prepare for the sourcing event in the Preparation phase.

In Preparation phase, you have to plan the entire sourcing event - list of suppliers and stakeholders, set the milestones (deadlines), prepare the set of questions to be asked to the suppliers, set the conditions that affects the quotes provided by the suppliers, and set the criteria to evaluate the responses.

You can activate an event once you add sourcing event details. But, in order to publish the event, you must add Suppliers and Milestones. However, we suggest you to publish the event after you complete the Preparation phase.

Tip:

Use Case

Sam prepares for the Sourcing Event in this phase.

Sam Invites the suppliers and sets the Milestones.

Then he adds Stakeholders, creates RFI, creates RFQ.

He then adds Price Modifiers and Evaluation Weightage.

You can perform the following tasks depending on the Sourcing Event Type used.

  • You must select Suppliers to invite.
  • You must define Milestones.
  • You must select Stakeholders.
  • You can create an RFI.
  • You can create an RFQ.
  • You can specify Price Modifiers.
  • You can specify an Evaluation Weightage.
Note:

Note

You can click Save once you complete entering details in various tabs under Preparation tab.

  • Adding the Suppliers
  • Adding the Milestones
  • Adding the Stakeholders
    Stakeholders, the internal members of the organization who organize the sourcing event, participate in the survey to rate the shortlisted suppliers on different performance indicators. The Stakeholders provide their opinion on the suppliers by Stakeholder Survey. Stakeholders must evaluate the suppliers on a neutral basis.
  • Managing Request for Information
    Request for Information (RFI) is a questionnaire prepared by the buyer to ask the questions to the suppliers.
  • Managing Request for Quotation
    Request for Quotation (RFQ) is for collecting the pricing quotes from the suppliers for the products or services that the buyer wants to procure.
  • Managing Request for Proposal
  • Adding Price Modifiers
    Price Modifiers are the conditions that you can include in RFQ to see the difference in total pricing of the supplier in presence of constraints or flexibility.
  • Adding Evaluation Weightage
    The Buyer has to set an Evaluation Weightage on the basis of which the scores are calculated. Evaluation Weightage is utilised in calculating the weighted score of the entire sourcing event.
