Managing the Surveys

The Surveys are the surveys done by the Stakeholders on the RFQs.

The Buyer can view the list of surveys generated by the Stakeholders for each supplier. The Buyer can also view the detail of each survey. The Stakeholder fills the Scorecard as a part of the survey. The Evaluation will be done on the basis ofsurvey. The scorecard assesses performance and satisfaction of buyer with individual suppliers. It compares suppliers across the categories. The scorecard provides objective measurements of performance and indicates the supplierâ€™s conformance to requirements.

In our example, Sam has selected Chatter Expert as the Stakeholder. Hence, after receiving all responses, Chatter Expert will receive a scorecard for each shortlisted supplier. He has to evaluate the responses and score the suppliers across all categories mentioned in the scorecard.

To start a survey

  1. Click the Sourcing Events tab.
  2. Select the appropriate sourcing event and click Edit.
  3. Click Start Survey.
    Note:

    Note

    Skip this step if you have selected Auto Mode as your Event Mode Type in your sourcing event type. For Auto Mode, the phase progression is carried out automatically, based on the date and time mentioned in Milestones.

  4. Click the Responses tab and click the Survey tab.
  5. Click Evaluate.
  6. Enter the scores under the Scores column, for each score metric.
  7. Click Save to save your scorecard.
  8. Click Submit Scores to submit the scores to your buyer.
  9. On the Sourcing Event page, the Survey tab will show the results.
  10. Click Compare to compare the results of Survey.
  11. To download the Survey Responses, click Download.
    Note:

    Survey Responses.xls file will start downloading immediately. To view the downloaded file, click Survey Responses.xls .

  12. Click Back to Sourcing Event.
  13. Click Save.
  14. Click Complete Survey.
    Note:

    Note

    Skip this step if you have selected Auto Mode as your Event Mode Type in your sourcing event type. For Auto Mode, the phase progression is carried out automatically, based on the date and time mentioned in Milestones.

Note:

Note

A Phase Progression pop-up is displayed for the confirmation of completing the phase. Click Yes to proceed to next phase. Click No to continue working in the same phase.
