Supplier Review and Approvals

Supplier Review and Approvals enables buyers to review and approve the newly created prospective supplier requests. For the suppliers that you have approved, an account will be created. Chevrons display the progress Approval process on Supplier Approvals screen. The table below describes the stages of Supplier Approvals.

Stage

Description

The supplier registration is in draft phase.

Review

The supplier registration is in review phase.

Approval

The supplier registration is in approval phase.

Create Supplier

The supplier is created in this phase.

The buyers can review the prospective supplier requests and supplier self-registration requests as well. The buyers can edit, review, and reject the requests. If the supplier's request is not up to the mark, the buyer can reject the request in the Review stage. You can edit the prospective supplier request and make the necessary changes, if needed. The prospective supplier request is then sent for an approval.

For the prospective supplier requests, if the request is approved, Sam can go ahead and integrate those suppliers with the portal. This can be done by clicking Create Supplier button in that prospective supplier request. If the prospective supplier request is rejected, Sam cannot proceed further with the request. If the prospective supplier request is cancelled, Sam cannot take any further action. Sam cannot even edit the request.

To submit a supplier for approval

  1. Click the Prospective Suppliers tab.
  2. Select the supplier that you want to approve.
  3. Click Submit for Approval to submit the prospective supplier request for approval.

    -or-

    Click Edit to edit the prospective supplier request.

    -or-

    Click Cancel to cancel the request and go back to the list of prospective supplier requests.

    -or-

    Click Reject to reject the prospective supplier request.

  4. On the Confirm Supplier Group screen, select a supplier group that you want to assign this supplier to, for Set Supplier Group to picklist menu.
    Tip:

    Supplier Group enables you to categorize the suppliers. You can then assign surveys to entire supplier group. For example, you are disinfecting wipes manufacturer; you can categorize the suppliers by adding them to Fabric Supplier Group, Chlorine Supplier Group, and Disinfectant Supplier Group. You can then add relevant suppliers in each group.

  5. Click Ok.

For simplicity purpose, it is a single-button click process for the buyers. However, you can make it advanced by integrating standard Salesforce Approvals or Apttus Advanced Approvals. Apttus allows you to configure approval processes using Salesforce Approvals or Apttus Advanced Approvals.

To approve or reject a supplier

  1. Click the Prospective Suppliers tab.
  2. Select the supplier that you want to approve.
  3. Click Approve to approve the supplier request.

    -or-

    Click Reject to reject the supplier request.

    -or-

    Click Cancel to cancel the supplier request.

Tip:

You can also approve or reject a supplier by performing the following steps.

  1. Click the Home tab.
  2. Scroll down to Items to Approve related list.
  3. Click Approve/Reject to approve or reject the supplier.

    -or-

    Click Reassign to reassign the supplier request.

  4. Add Comments, if any.
  5. Click Approve to approve the supplier request.

    -or-

    Click Reject to reject the supplier request.

    -or-

    Click Cancel to cancel the supplier request.

You have reviewed and approved the request. Next, you must create the supplier account. For more information on creating supplier accounts, refer to Supplier Portal Integration.
