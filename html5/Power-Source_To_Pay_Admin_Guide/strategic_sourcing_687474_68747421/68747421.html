Strategic Sourcing

Strategic Sourcing is a procurement process to select the most appropriate source of supply (suppliers) for a set of products or services. Strategic Sourcing enables you to source your products from the suppliers and improve supplier decisions by performing an early analysis. You can avoid supplier risks and drive smarter spend with multi-stage Rfx. A guided Wizard makes it easy for you to source your products from best suppliers.

Benefits of using Strategic Sourcing:

  • Selecting the best-qualified supplier for your deal or agreement.
  • Automate the sourcing cycles.
  • Streamline the sourcing process.
  • Easy and quick creation of RFIs and RFQs.
  • Easy negotiations.
  • Automated Phase progressions with displayed Status.
  • Faster Procurement process.
  • Bird's eye view of entire sourcing process
  • An Intuitive User Interface.
  • Integrated with Apttus CLM.
  • Ability to create Supplier Communities

An organization can use Strategic Sourcing to manage the entire buy-side procurement process including inviting the suppliers, preparing RFI and RFQ, Evaluating the suppliers with graphical information, and awarding the deal to the best supplier(s). Strategic Sourcing comes into picture once you have on-boarded the suppliers. You can perform the process of Strategic Sourcing using the Sourcing Events.
