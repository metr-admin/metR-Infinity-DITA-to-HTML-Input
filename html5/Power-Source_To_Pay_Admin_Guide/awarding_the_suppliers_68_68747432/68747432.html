Awarding the Suppliers

Awards are presented to the suppliers that are shortlisted for the business transaction.

Based on the events, the participants of the sourcing event are notified using emails having specific templates. Awards can be reopened. The buyer can change the award winners. Email notifications are sent to the previous winners that the winner has been changed. Email notifications are sent to new winners.

Award tab will be visible to the winners only. For the suppliers who have lost the sourcing event, the Award tab will be grayed. Email notifications are sent to both winning suppliers and loosing suppliers.

To award the Supplier

  1. Click the Sourcing Events tab.
  2. Select the appropriate sourcing event and click Edit.
  3. Click Start Award.
    Note:

    Note

    Skip this step if you have selected Auto Mode as your Event Mode Type in your sourcing event type. For Auto Mode, the phase progression is carried out automatically, based on the date and time mentioned in Milestones.

  4. Click the Award tab and click Award to select the winner.
  5. Select the appropriate winner on the basis of comparison shown in the graphs.
  6. To enter a comment for Award Justification, enter your comment in the Comments text box and click Add.

    -or-

    Click No Comments to continue without entering any comment.

  7. Click Save.
  8. The results are displayed as shown in the image below, on the Award tab.
  9. Click Complete Award.
    Note:

    Note

    Skip this step if you have selected Auto Mode as your Event Mode Type in your sourcing event type. For Auto Mode, the phase progression is carried out automatically, based on the date and time mentioned in Milestones.

  10. Click Close Event.

    Note:

    Note

    Skip this step if you have selected Auto Mode as your Event Mode Type in your sourcing event type. For Auto Mode, the phase progression is carried out automatically, based on the date and time mentioned in Milestones.

  11. Click Save.
    Note:

    Note

    A Phase Progression pop-up is displayed for the confirmation of completing the phase. Click Yes to proceed to next phase. Click No to continue working in the same phase.

    Note:

    Note

    You can now see the updated Chevrons, Event Status, Phase and Phase Status.
