Managing Consolidated Scorecards

Consolidated Scorecards are integrated statistical reports that keep track of the quantitative and qualitative performance of all your suppliers. These integrated scorecards include only the completed scorecards of all the suppliers from all scorers for a particular period. Apttus STP displays the results in graphical information using charts. The scores can be grouped by Scorers or Metrics. You can view the results on Radar Chart or Bar Chart. Also, the charts can be sorted by Metrics or Categories.

The Consolidated scorecards are not stored in the system. Hence, when you click View for a supplier, a consolidated view, using only completed scorecards, is generated at that time.

Benefits of using Consolidated Scorecards:

  • To access the integrated information of all your suppliers at one place.
  • To view the scores of your suppliers using graphs.
  • To plan a strategy, as an organization, based on the scores.
  • To monitor the growth of your organization.
  • To monitor the performance of all your suppliers.
Note:

Note

Only the scorecards that are completely scored (Status is Completed) are taken into consideration while generating a view for Consolidated Scorecards.

Tip:

Use Case

Sam, the Procurement Manager, does not have enough time to view the individual scorecards of all his suppliers. Instead, he views the consolidated scorecards to get a run-time access to the performance of his suppliers. Sam can either group the suppliers by the score metrics that he has mentioned, or he can group the suppliers the scorers.

To view a consolidated scorecard

  1. Click the Master Scorecards tab.
  2. Select the master scorecard for which you want a consolidated view of.
  3. Click the Status tab.
  4. Click View for the supplier for whom you want view the consolidated scorecard.
    Note:

    Note

    The scores of only completed scorecards (Status : Completed) is displayed in Consolidated Scorecards.

  5. Under the Information section, general information of the supplier is displayed.
  6. Under the Scorecard Detail section, the results are displayed in chart format. Select the type of chart from Chart Type picklist menu. The available options are.

    Options

    Description

    Bar Chart
    Select this option to display the scores in a bar chart from.

    Radar Chart
    Select this option to display the scores in a radar chart form.
  7. Select the type of parameter to group your scores by, from View By picklist menu.

    Option

    Description

    Metrics

    Select this option to display the scores of your suppliers grouped by Score Metrics.

    Categories

    Select this option to display the scores of your suppliers grouped by Score Categories.
  8. To view the scores by metric, click the Score by Metric tab.
  9. To view the scores by scorers, click the Score by Scorers tab.
  10. Scorer List provides you the information of scorers and their individual scorecards. You can view the individual scorecards as well.

    Click View for the scorer whose scorecard you want to view.
