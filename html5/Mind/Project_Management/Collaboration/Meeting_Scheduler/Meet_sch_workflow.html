Meeting Scheduler Workflow Stages

For information on setting a workflow stage to the next stage, refer to Common Application Features, Workflow Stage Transitions.
Phase Current Workflow Status Action Subsequent Workflow Status Comments
1 Draft Save Draft Indicates the Meeting request is in the draft status and not published.
Publish Published Indicates the meeting request is published.
2 Published Cancel Meeting Cancelled Indicates the meeting request is cancelled.
3 Cancelled - - Cancelled meetings cannot be published.
