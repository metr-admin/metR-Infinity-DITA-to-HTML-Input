Attachments

You can upload attachments, or link files in the Documents folders to every form and workflow transition. There is no limitation on the number of attachments or the size of attachments that you can upload in a form or during workflow transition.

Refer to the following topics to upload or link, and access attachments:
  • Attaching Files to a Form
  • Accessing Attachments
