Initiating a Ball in Court Notification

  • Notifications templates are configured for the form in the workflow stage when the notifications is being initiated. For information on configuring email templates, refer to Configuring Ball in Court Notifications .

You can share form information with a user invited to the project but not have access to the form or not be a stakeholder at the workflow stage of the form when the notification is being initiated.

On initiating a Ball in Court notification, an email is sent to the recipient and thenotification is saved in theINBOXpage of the recipient.

The following procedure uses the Expenses form as an example to describe the procedure to initiatethe Ball in Court notification.

  1. In the module menu, click Projects.
    The PROJECTS list page is displayed.
  2. In the module menu, click Planning.
  3. In the navigation pane, click Planned Projects.
    The PLANNED PROJECTSlist page is displayed.
  4. In the list page, double-click the required project.
  5. In the navigation pane, expand the project folder, expand the Commitments & Expenses folder, and then click Expenses Form.
  6. Click New.
  7. Click Miscellaneous Expenses, specify the expense details, and then click Save.
  8. Select the saved form, and then click Edit.
  9. Click Workflow, and then click Ball in Court.
    The feature is available only if the form is in the workflow stage for which a template is selected in the Ball In Court Request Email Template list in the workflow definition of the form. For information on configuring email templates, refer to Configuring Ball in Court Notifications
  10. From the Route To drop-down list, select the user to share the information of the required form.
    Available options are users invited to the project.
  11. In the Notes box, enter any notes to be sent to the recipient.
  12. In the Set Days To Complete for Next Stage box, enter the number of days by when the task must be completed.
  13. Click OK.
    A notification email is sent to the recipient, and a notification is saved in the INBOX page of the user.

    The recipient can now respond to the Ball in Court notification.

    Information that is shared is based on the template selected in the Ball In Court Request Email Template list in the workflow definition of the form.
