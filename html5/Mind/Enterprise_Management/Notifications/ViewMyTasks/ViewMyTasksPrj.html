Viewing My Tasks at the Project Level

  1. In the module menu, click Projects.
    The PROJECTS list page is displayed.
    Alternatively, perform the following steps:
    1. In the module menu, click Planning.
      The PLANNING DASHBOARD is displayed.
    2. In the navigation pane, click Planned Projects.
      The PLANNED PROJECTS list page is displayed.
  2. In the list page, double-click the required project to open the project.
  3. In the navigation pane, expand the project folder, and then click My Tasks.
    Note:
    The number of tasks pending on you is displayed adjacent to the My Tasks label in the navigation pane.

    From the MY TASKS page, you can access records with a pending task. For more information on completing a pending task, refer to Completing a Pending Task.
