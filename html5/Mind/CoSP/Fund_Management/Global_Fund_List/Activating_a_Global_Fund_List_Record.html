Activating a Global Fund List Record

  • The role of the logged-in user should be Fund Manager.

You can activate an inactive Global Fund List record to enable access for project fund usage.

  1. In the module menu, click Home.
    The Enterprise Dashboard is displayed.
  2. In the navigation pane, expand the Fund Management folder, and then click Global Fund List.
    The GLOBAL FUND LIST page is displayed.
  3. In the list page, select the appropriate record to activate.
  4. To activate a record, perform either of the following:

    • In the toolbar, click Mark Active/Inactive, and then click Mark Active.

    • In the toolbar, click Edit, select the Is Active check box, and then click Save & Exit

    The selected record is marked as active.
