Deactivating a Global Fund List Record

  • The role of the logged-in user should be Fund Manager.

You can deactivate an active Global Fund List record. If a fund source is marked as inactive, then the fund source will not be available for projects.
Note:
Deactivating a global fund source that is already assigned to a project does not change the assigned fund source data of the project.
  1. In the module menu, click Home.
    The Enterprise Dashboard is displayed.
  2. In the navigation pane, expand the Fund Management folder, and then click Global Fund List.
    The GLOBAL FUND LIST page is displayed.
  3. In the list page, select the appropriate record to deactivate, and then perform either of the following:

    • In the toolbar, click Mark Active/Inactive, and then click Mark Inactive.

    • In the toolbar, click Edit, clear the Is Active check box, and then click Save & Exit.

    The selected record is marked as inactive.
