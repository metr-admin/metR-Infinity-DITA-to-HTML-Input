Budget Management

The Budget Management module is a cost budgeting utility. It enables project practitioners to track the estimated budget of the associated project. Also, this provides the ability to determine the best spending plan for the project. The budget information is available in Mind through data integration with EBS.

Budget Estimates: The budget estimate displays the overall cost of project implementation. On receiving the project budget details from EBS into Mind, the Budget Estimates records are automatically created and approved.

Forecasts: Once the budget of the project is estimated, a forecast is automatically created and approved during data integration with EBS.

Budget Estimate Revision: The changes in budget are recorded in the Budget Estimate Revision form.

Current Budget Estimate: The page displays the latest approved budget estimate and all of its approved revisions.
