Viewing the Current Budget

The Current Budget Estimate page provides a consolidated view of all items that are part of approved budget estimate and all of its approved revisions.
Note:
If the project does not have any approved budget, then the CURRENT BUDGET ITEMS page is blank.
  1. In the module menu, click Projects
    The PROJECTS list page is displayed.
  2. In the list page, double-click the required project, and in the navigation pane, expand the project folder.
  3. In the navigation pane, expand Budget Management, and then click Current Budget .
    The CURRENT BUDGET ITEMS page is displayed.
    The Total Amount in $ field displays the sum of amounts of all items of the approved budget estimate and all of its revisions.
