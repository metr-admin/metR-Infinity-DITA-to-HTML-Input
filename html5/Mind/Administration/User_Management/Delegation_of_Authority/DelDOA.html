Deleting a Delegated Record

  • The record is in theAwaiting status.
You can delete a delegated record.
  1. In the module menu, clickAdministration.
    TheADMINISTRATIONpage is displayed.
  2. In the navigation pane, expandUser Management, and then click Delegation of Authority.
    TheDELEGATION OF AUTHORITY list page is displayed.
  3. Select the appropriate record, and then clickDelete.
