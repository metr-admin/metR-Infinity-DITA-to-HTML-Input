Viewing Delegation Details

  • The status of the record is either Awaiting or Delegated.
You can view delegation details.
  1. In the module menu, clickAdministration.
    TheADMINISTRATIONpage is displayed.
  2. In the navigation pane, expandUser Management, and then click Delegation of Authority.
    TheDELEGATION OF AUTHORITYlist page is displayed.
  3. Select the appropriate record, and then clickView.
    TheDELEGATION OF AUTHORITYpage is displayed.
    The Is Delegatedcheck box is selected by default for the delegated projects or contracts.
  4. ClickSave.
