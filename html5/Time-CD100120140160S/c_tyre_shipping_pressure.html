Standard tyre, ply rating and inflation pressures.

The operating inflation pressure is based on the weight of a ready-to-work machine without attachments, at rated payload, and in average operating conditions. Pressures for each application may vary and should always be obtained from your tyre supplier.

Note:

Fill tyres to the recommended pressures listed +35 kPa (5 psi). Tyres can be filled with nitrogen.
