Steering Mechanism - Check, Lubricate

Typical Example

  1. Lubricate the steer axle king pins, total of four fittings. Two on the right side and two on the left side.
  2. Lubricate the steering link bearings, total of four fittings. Two on the right side and two on the left side.
  3. Check for any worn or loose components of the steering mechanism. Remove any debris or trash as required.
  4. Lubricate the steer axle shaft pins, total of two fittings (front & rear).
