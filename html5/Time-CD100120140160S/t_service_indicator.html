Service Indicator

Typical Example

  1. Observe the air cleaner service indicator.
  2. Service the air cleaner when the RED band in the service indicator, locks in the visible position. See topic, “Air Intake System - Check, Clean” in “When Required”.
    Note:

    Service the element more frequently, as required, in severe dust or lint conditions. Also, service it more frequently, where the operator is required to wear a respirator.
