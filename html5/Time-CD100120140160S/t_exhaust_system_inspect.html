Exhaust System - Inspect

Inspection for leaks, damage

  1. Perform visual inspection of exhaust system.
  2. Inspect exhaust manifold for leaks.
  3. Inspect exhaust piping and connections for leaks.
  4. Inspect muffler inlet and outlet and leaks.
  5. Repair any/all leaks found.
