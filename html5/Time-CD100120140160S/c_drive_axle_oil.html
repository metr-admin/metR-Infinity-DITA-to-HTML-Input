Note:

Failure to follow the recommendation will cause shortened life due to excessive gear wear.

Select oil that meets below specifications.

  • API GL-5
  • MIL-L-2105 C, D

Gear Oil offers maximum protection against the scoring and pitting of gear teeth and rolling element bearings.

Gear Oil can also provide excellent stability under high temperature conditions and has superior low temperature performance. It will also give protection against rust and corrosion.
