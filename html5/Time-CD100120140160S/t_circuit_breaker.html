Circuit Breaker

Typical Example

  1. Raise the left hood cover.

    Typical Example

  2. The main circuit breaker is located on the rear of the support for the controls.
    Note:

    To reset circuit breakers push on the button. The button should stay in if the breaker is reset. If the button will not stay in, or comes out shortly after reset, have the circuits checked.
