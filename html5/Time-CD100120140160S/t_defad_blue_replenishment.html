DEF/Ad-Blue Replenishment

Notice:

Do not allow the lift truck to become low on DEF(Ad-Blue) or completely run out of /DEF (Ad-Blue).

  1. Sediment or other impurities in the DEF(Ad-Blue) Tank could be drawn into the after treatment.
  2. This could result in damage to components.
  3. Do not fill the DEF(Ad-Blue)tank to the top.
  4. DEF(Ad-Blue) Tank could be damaged because of volume expansion during DEF(Ad-blue) freezing in cold condition.

Typical Example

  1. Park the lift truck only at a designated safe location. Place the transmission in Neutral. Lower the forks on the ground. Apply the parking brake. Stop the engine.
  2. Using the start key, open the urea fluid tank door beside the frame. Open the blue urea fluid tank filler cap.

    Typical Example

  3. Fill the fuel tank slowly. Refer to the section on ‘Fuel and Urea fluid Refill Volume.”
  4. After the refill, close the urea fluid tank cap firmly. Remove spilt urea fluid, if any, with an adsorbent.
CAUTION:

LOW DEF/Ad-Blue LEVEL WILL DISTURB AFTER TREATMENT FOR TIER-4 EXHAUST GAS EMISSION, AND CAB BE CAUSE OF SERIOUS DAMAGE TO ENGINE AND SYSTEM.
