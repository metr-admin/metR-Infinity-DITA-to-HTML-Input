Lubricant Information

Some classifications and abbreviations we use in this section follow S.A.E. (Society of Automotive Engineers) J754 nomenclature and others follow S.A.E. J183.

All MIL specifications are U.S.A. Military.

Recommended oil viscosities are given in the “Lubricant Viscosities” chart later in this section of the manual.

Greases are classified according to the National Lubricating Grease Institute (NLGI) based on ASTM D217-68 worked Penetration characteristics which give a defined consistency number.
