LPG

LPG is poisonous and flammable.

Breathing LPG vapors or repeated contact of LPG with skin can cause personal injury.

Use LPG only in well-ventilated areas.

Do not smoke while changing LPG cylinders. Use LPG with care to avoid fires.

Do not store replacement LPG cylinders in living areas or in the operator's compartment.

Do not store LPG cylinders in direct sunlight or at temperatures above 39°C (102 °F).

Discard cylinders in a safe place. Do not puncture or burn cylinders.

Keep LPG cylinders out of the reach of unauthorised personnel.
