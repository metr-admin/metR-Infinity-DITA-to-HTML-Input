Lift Fork Adjustment

Warning:

When adjusting the fork spread, be careful not to pinch your hand between forks and the carriage slot.

For load stability, always adjust the forks as wide as possible. Position the load evenly on both forks.

Typical Example of Shaft type Fork

  1. Move up the pin in each fork to slide the fork on the carriage bar.
  2. Adjust the forks in the position most appropriate for the load and as wide as possible for load stability.
  3. When adjusting the forks, make sure that the weight of the load is centreed on the truck.
  4. After adjustment, set the pins to keep the forks in place.
    Warning:

    Make sure the forks are locked before carrying a load.
