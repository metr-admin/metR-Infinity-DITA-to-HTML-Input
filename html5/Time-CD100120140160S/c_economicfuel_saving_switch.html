Economic(Fuel saving) switch

This switch is to keep the engine’s speed from exceeding a limit.

With the switch off, the engine reaches the maximum speed when the accelerator pedal is fully pressed. With the switch on, the engine’s speed is limited to a maximum rpm of 2,100. In the case where the vehicle is used mainly for driving, using this switch reduces fuel consumption by approximately 10%.
