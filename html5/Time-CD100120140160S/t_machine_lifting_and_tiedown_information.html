Machine Lifting and Tiedown Information

Notice:

Improper lifting or tiedowns can allow load to shift and cause injury and/or damage.

  1. Weight is given on the serial plate.
  2. Use proper rated cables and slings for lifting. Position the crane for level lift truck lift.
  3. Spreader bar widths should be sufficient to prevent contact with the lift truck
  4. Use the tiedown locations provided for lift truck tiedown.

    Check the state and local laws governing weight, width, and length of a load.

    Contact your Time Lift Truck branch for shipping instructions for your lift truck.
