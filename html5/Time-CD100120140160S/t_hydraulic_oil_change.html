Hydraulic Oil - Change

Warning:

Hot oil and components can cause personal injury. Do not allow hot oil or components to contact skin.

Lower the forks and part the lift truck on level ground. Park the lift truck where there is sufficient space for cabin tilting. With the mast in vertical position, engage the parking brake, set the gear shift lever at Neutral, and stop the engine.

  1. Tilt the cabin completely.
  2. Open the hydraulic oil cap slowly.
  3. Remove the hydraulic tank drain plug located on the bottom of the frame. Allow the oil to drain in a suitable container. Clean and install the plug.
  4. Fill the hydraulic oil tank with oil. See the section, “Refill Capacities.”
  5. Install the hydraulic oil cap.
  6. Start the engine and operate the hydraulic controls, and the steering system, through a few cycles to fill the lines. Look for oil leaks.
  7. Stop the engine and check the oil level. With all the cylinders withdrawn, check that the oil level is in the red band of the level gauge.
