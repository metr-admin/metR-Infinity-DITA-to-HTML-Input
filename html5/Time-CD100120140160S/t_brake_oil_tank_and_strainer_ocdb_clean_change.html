Brake Oil Tank and Strainer (OCDB) - Clean, Change

Warning:

Hot oil and components can cause personal injury. Do not allow hot oil or components to contact skin.

Park the lift truck level with the forks lowered, parking brake engaged, transmission in NEUTRAL and the engine stopped

  1. Place an appropriate container under the brake oil tank to catch the oil as it drains. Remove the drain plug under the brake oil tank.

    Typical Example

  2. Clean and reinstall the drain plug.
  3. Remove hydraulic hose and bolts from the brake oil tank side cover at the frame side.

    Typical Example

  4. Disassemble the strainer and wash them in clean, nonflammable solvent.
  5. Assemble the strainer and connect the hydraulic hose.

  7. Start the engine and check the oil dipstick.
  8. Check for oil leaks at the brake oil tank side cover and drain plug.
  9. Stop the engine
