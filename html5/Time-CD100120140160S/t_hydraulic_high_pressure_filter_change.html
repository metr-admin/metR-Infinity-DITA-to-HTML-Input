Hydraulic High Pressure Filter - Change

Park the lift truck with the forks lowered, parking brake engaged, transmission in NEUTRAL and the engine stopped.

  1. Remove the floor plate or tilt the cabin
  2. Remove filter housing ②.
  3. Remove and discard filter element from filter housing ②.
  4. Clean the filter housing with a clean, nonflammable solvent.
  5. Insert a new filter element into the filter housing
  6. Inspect the filter housing seal. Replace if necessary.
  7. Apply a small amount of clean oil to the filter element seal and housing seal.
  8. Install the filter housing ② with filter to the housing base ① and tighten 40 to 46 N·m (30 to 35 lbs·ft).
  9. Start the engine and step on the acceleration pedal. Look for oil leaks.
  10. Stop the engine and check the oil level. With all cylinders retracted, maintain the oil level to the FULL mark on the dip stick/filler cap assembly
