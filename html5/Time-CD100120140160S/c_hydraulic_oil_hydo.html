Hydraulic Oil (HYDO)

The following commercial classifications can be used in the hydraulic system.

  • ISO 6743/4 HM
  • AFNOR NFE 48-603 HM
  • DIN 51524 TEIL 2 H-LP
  • HAGGLUNDS DENISON HFO-HF2
  • CINCINNATI P68, 69, 70

    Viscosity : ISO VG32

Industrial premium hydraulic oils that have passed the Vickers vane pump test (35VQ25). These oils should have antiwear, antifoam, antirust and antioxidation additives for heavy duty use as stated by the oil supplier. ISO viscosity grade of 32 would normally be selected.
