SCR system tampering for USA / Europe

There are several tampering level points at which the DEF indicator lamp lights up or blinks and the display shows a message to warn that the DEF/Ad-Blue is not being consumed due to the malfunctioning of the SCR system, the installation of another device to the system, or the handling of related parts. The lower the point, the more the system limits the engine power and speed.
