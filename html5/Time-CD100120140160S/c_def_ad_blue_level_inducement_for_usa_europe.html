DEF/Ad-Blue Level inducement for USA / Europe

There are several DEF/Ad-Blue level points at which the DEF level indicator lamp changes and the display shows a message to warn the shortage of DEF/Ad-Blue. The lower the point, the more the system limits the engine power and speed in order to prevent nitrogen oxide emissions from exceeding the acceptable level due to a lack of DEF/Ad-Blue.
