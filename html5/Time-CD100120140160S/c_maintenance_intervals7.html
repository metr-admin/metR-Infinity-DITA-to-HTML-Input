Maintenance Intervals

Notice:

Never exceed the Maintenance Intervals specified in the manual. Defects and/or damage to the important functional components may be resulted in.

Notice:

All maintenance and repair, except every 10 service hours or daily, on the lift truck must be performed by qualified and authorised personnel only.

Notice:

Careless disposal of waste oil can harm the environment and can be dangerous to persons. Always dispose of waste oil to authorised personnel only.

  • When Required
  • Every 10 Service Hours or Daily
  • First 50-100 Service Hours or a Week
  • Every 500 Service Hours or 3 Months
  • Every 1000 Service Hours or 6 Months
  • Every 1500 Service Hours or 9 Months
  • Every 2000 Service Hours or Yearly
  • Every 4500 Service Hours or 36 Months
