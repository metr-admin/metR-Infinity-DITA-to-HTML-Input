Mast, Carriage, Lift Chains & Attachments - Inspect, Lubricate

  1. Operate the lift, tilt and attachment controls. Listen for unusual noises. These may indicate a need for repair.
  2. Check for loose bolts and nuts on the carriage. Remove any debris from the carriage and mast.
  3. Check the forks and attachments for free operation and damage. Have repairs made if necessary.

    Typical Example

  4. Brush a film of oil on all links of the chain.
  5. Raise and lower the carriage a few times to allow lubricant to enter into the chain links.
    Warning:

    Lubricate chains more frequently than normal in applications where the lift truck is operating in an atmosphere which could cause corrosion of components or when lift truck must work in rapid lift cycles.

  6. Check the air cleaner housing for loose latches.
  • Lubricate Mast Side Rollers
