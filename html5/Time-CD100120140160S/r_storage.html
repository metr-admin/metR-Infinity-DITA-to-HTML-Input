Storage

The shelf life of DEF/Ad-Blue varies by storage temperature.

Between 40° F and 80° F is recommended to maintain shelf life.

Above 12° F is recommended to avoid crystallization, which starts at 11° F.

Below 86° F is recommended, which cause hydrolysis to occur, with the consequent formation of ammonia and pressure rise, and will reduce shelf life.
