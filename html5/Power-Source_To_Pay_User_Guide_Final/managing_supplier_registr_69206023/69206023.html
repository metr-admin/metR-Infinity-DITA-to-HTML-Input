Managing Supplier Registration

Supplier Registration is a process to register suppliers in the system. The prospective suppliers can register themselves as a candidate to the buyer company in two ways.

  • Supplier Self-RegistrationYou can register yourself using the link provided in email or on your client's website.
  • Prospective Supplier Request

    A supplier or internal member of your buyer will do the registration for you. Contact your buyer for sending him a prospective supplier request.

You, as a supplier, can click on the link provided by your buyer and register as a supplier in the buyer's system.

Parent topic: Introduction

Supplier Self-Registration

A Supplier Self-Registration is a type of supplier registration where a supplier registers himself on the company's website by himself. Supplier Self-Registration is majorly used by the suppliers to register themselves with the buyers. These registrations are then evaluated and approved by Stakeholders. The approved suppliers are provided login access to supplier portal. After you get your login access, your compliance is checked using the compliance surveys.

To self-register a supplier

  1. Click the link on your buyer's website or in the email that you have received.
  2. On the General Information tab, complete the following fields.

    Field Name

    Description

    Company Name

    Enter Name of your company. This field is mandatory.

    Contact Name

    Enter the Name of the contact person. This field is mandatory.

    Tax ID

    Enter the Tax ID of your company.

    Phone Number

    Enter the Phone Number of your company. This field is mandatory.

    Fax Number

    Enter the Fax Number of your company.

    Email

    Enter the Email Address of your company. This field is mandatory.

    Website

    Enter the Web Address of your company.

    Address 1

    Enter the Address of your company.

    Address 2

    Enter the Address of your company.

    Country

    Select the Country.

    State

    Select the State.

    City

    Select the City.

    Zip Code

    Enter your Zip Code.

    Product and Service Location

    Industry

    Select the Industry of your company.

    Commodity

    Select the Commodity of your company.

    Region

    Select the Region of your company.

    Comments

    Enter Comments, if any.
  3. Click Upload to attach a document.
    Note:

    For the Attachments in Apttus STP, you can upload any file. We allow files of the following extension types: (.PDF), (.RTF), (.RTX), (.TXT), (.JPG), (.JPEG), (.PNG), (.GIF), (.TIF), (.BMP), (.DOC), (.DOCX), (.DOCM), (.PPT), (.PPTX), (.PPTM), (.PPS), (.PPSX), (.XLS), (.XLSX), (.XLSM), (.ZIP), (.MP3), (.M4A), (.OGG), (.WAV), (.MP4), (.M4V), (.MOV), (.OGV), (.3GP), (.3G2). The application might become slow if you upload a file higher than 25 MB.

  4. Select the file that you want to attach, and click Open.
  5. To delete an existing file, click the icon.
    Note:

    You can access the existing attachments if your buyer has uploaded any. You can not delete the attachments uploaded by your buyer. You can delete the attachments uploaded by you only.

  6. Click Next and respond to all the surveys visible on the tabs.
    Note:

    If you do not respond to all surveys that are assigned to you, an error message is displayed: You must fill up all the surveys first.

  7. Provide the information on all tabs and then click Submit.
