To accept or decline a sourcing event

  1. Click the Sourcing Events tab. All the events you are invited to are displayed here.
  2. Click View for the sourcing event that you want to open.
  3. If your supplier has opted for NDA Required option, you will see Non-Disclosure Agreement screen.
  4. To download the Agreement, click Here. Review all the terms and conditions mentioned in the agreement.
  5. If you agree to all the terms and conditions set by the buyer, click Accept.
  6. Click Accept to accept the invitation to sourcing event. In order to decline the invitation to the sourcing event, click Reject.
  7. Click Back to Event List.
