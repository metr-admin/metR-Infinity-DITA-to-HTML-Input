Batch House Personnel Training

Purpose

To develop a procedure that applies to all plants for proper training of Batch House personnel.

Reference

  • BAT-02-003Batch House Environmental, Health and Safety
  • BAT-02-004Batch House Housekeeping
  • BAT-02-021Batch House Personnel Responsibilities

Document Classification

  • Organization and Training
  • Safety and Environment

Fundamentals

  • FUR-603

Procedure

  1. All Batch House personnel shall be properly trained for their specific responsibilities defined in the following documents:
    1. BAT-02-003 Batch House Environmental, Health and Safety
    2. BAT-02-004 Batch House Housekeeping
    3. BAT-02-021 Batch House Personnel Responsibilities
  2. The Batch & Furnace Manager shall have a training plan for Batch House personnel responsibilities, which shall be reviewed annually by Human Resources (HR) and/or the department supervisor/manager.
  3. Formal training materials (electronic or hard copy) shall be part of the active training program.
    1. Manufacturing Fundamentals Materials: Fundamentals Policy, Work Instructions, Master Specification and Design Requirements (MSDR), training videos/materials, etc.
    2. Regionally developed training materials, not in conflict with the Manufacturing Fundamentals, related to specific plant requirements.
    3. Training shall be conducted by trainers qualified to deliver training for individual equipment or tasks.
    4. Training performed by, including but not limited to:
      1. Global or furnace network subject matter expert(s).
      2. Experienced Batch & Furnace Manager.
      3. Third party expert with Network Leader approval.
    5. New Batch House personnel may be paired with a qualified mentor trainer for on-the-job training to reach the required levels.
  4. Documentation of training records shall be maintained in the plantâ€™s training database or equivalent.
  5. The Plant Manager, Batch & Furnace Manager and Human Resources (HR) shall be accountable for implementing the Batch House specific training for all Batch House personnel.
