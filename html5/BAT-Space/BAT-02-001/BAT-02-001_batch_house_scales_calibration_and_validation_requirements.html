Batch House Scales Calibration and Validation Requirements

Purpose

To define a procedure to perform the batch house scale calibrations and validations for an Space plant.

Reference

  • BAT-03-031 Critical Load Cells Monthly Maintenance
  • BAT-03-032 Critical Load Cells Calibration
  • BAT-03-038 Batch House Scales Validation
  • BAT-07-041 Batch Systems Maintenance

Document Classification

  • Asset Protection and Stability
  • Efficiency and Throughput
  • Quality

Fundamentals

  • GLS-114
  • SYS-308

Procedure

  1. Batch house scale calibrations shall be performed by accredited external vendors at the frequency specified in MSDR BAT-07-041 Batch Systems Maintenance, as described in Work Instruction BAT-03-032 Critical Load Cells Calibration.
  2. All scale validations shall be performed at the frequency specified in MSDR BAT-07-041 Batch Systems Maintenance with the appropriate validation weight, as described in Work Instruction BAT-03-038 Batch House Scales Validation.
    1. The plant shall validate the scale up to the maximum weight that will be measured by the scale (this can be performed in steps), according to the batch formula used at the plant.
    2. If this validation is made by the plant:
      1. The plant shall maintain validation weights and make them available.
      2. The validation weights shall be certified on a yearly basis and records proving the performance of these certifications shall be maintained.
  3. Additionally, the plant should follow the procedures presented in Work Instruction BAT-03-031 Critical Load Cells Monthly Maintenance.
