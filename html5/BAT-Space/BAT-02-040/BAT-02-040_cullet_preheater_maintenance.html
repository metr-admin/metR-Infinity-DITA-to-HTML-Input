Cullet Preheater Maintenance

Purpose

To define a procedure that applies to all plants for proper maintenance of the cullet preheater system.

Reference

  • BAT-02-030 Cullet Preheater Operation
  • FUR-02-001 Furnace Safety
  • BAT-03-009 Cullet Preheater Cleaning
  • BAT-04-030 Cullet Preheater Operation and Maintenance Manual - v6
  • BAT-07-040 Cullet Preheater Maintenance Conditions

Procedure

  1. Plant or Third Party personnel shall follow and be familiar with Procedure FUR-02-001 Furnace Safety.
  2. Plant or Third Party personnel shall be trained according to Training Material BAT-04-030 Cullet Preheater Operation and Maintenance Manual - v6.
  3. Plant or Third Party personnel shall inspect and maintain the cullet preheater system according to the requirements and frequencies specified within MSDR BAT-07-040 Cullet Preheater Maintenance Conditions.
  4. The cullet preheater system shall be cleaned according to Work Instruction BAT-03-009 Cullet Preheater Cleaning.
  5. All spares identified in MSDR BAT-07-040 Cullet Preheater Maintenance Conditions shall be maintained and on site for use in case of failure.
  6. All personnel shall notify their direct supervisor before performing any activities on the cullet preheater system or ancillary equipment.
  7. When performing any maintenance tasks, personnel shall follow the approved method for starting and stopping the cullet preheater as defined in Procedure BAT-02-030 Cullet Preheater Operation.
  8. Emergency dilution fan(s) shall always be operationally ready, set to automatic, and connected to a backup power system.
  9. Space policy for permit-required confined spaces shall be followed.
    1. The cullet preheater is considered a permit-required confined space.
    2. The cullet silo is considered a permit-required confined space.
    3. The batch silo is considered a permit-required confined space.
    4. The ductwork is considered a permit-required confined space.
  10. Inspection ports shall not be opened during on-line (hot) operation unless following the method specified in Work Instruction BAT-03-009 Cullet Preheater Cleaning.
  11. Only authorized personnel shall manually override automatic valves.
