FSC and PRS Support Escalation Procedure

Purpose

  1. To define a process to Space plants to assess system issues for FSC and PRS.
  2. To define a process for Space plants to escalate technical support for FSC and PRS when needed

Application

  1. This process applies to all Space plants that have FSC and PRS technology. Licensee plants should contact their Space Global Technical Assistance Account Manager.
  2. FSC and PRS are High Risk Systems obsolescence ranked – Unsupportable systems. The reason is that most (if not all) spare parts are not available for those systems. Plants must rely on in-house inventories, and because of the age of this equipment, in-house spare parts should not be considered a reliable solution.

Document Classification

Definitions

FSC: Forming Supervisory Computer
This is the main computer server for the forming electronic system for the Space IS Machines
PRS: Production Recording System
This server is the one that consolidates the information from the cold end from Space inspection machines and losses

Procedure

  1. System (PRS or FSC) issue has been assessed and reference material is used by PNL to go over first tier of the troubleshooting.
  2. PNL uses the Manufacturing DB to initiate escalation process and involves Discipline Network Leader.
  3. DNL takes and maintains ownership of the incident, verifies all technical review done by the plant. Updates the incident information, as needed.
  4. DNL works with Global Network / other DNLs to brainstorm the issue.
  5. DNL coordinates technical reviews with GNL and R, andamp;D until achieving final resolution.
  6. DNL clearly explains the resolution and closes the service request.
