Purpose

  1. To define a work instruction describing how to diagnosis and fix a failure with a Digi Edgeport that has an external power supply
  2. These supplies have been found to intermittently fail to deliver the necessary voltage for the Edgeport to function. When this occurs, the Edgeport stops communicating to the Integrity computer, and communications problems with the COM-SOC 1 machine timing systems follows. Communication is typically restored by rebooting the FSC Integrity server.
