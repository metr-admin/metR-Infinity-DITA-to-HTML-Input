GCT GK25 Engine

  1. Loosen the air relief plug on top of the water pump.
  2. Open the radiator cap and infuse the cooling water.
  3. Tighten the air relief plug when the cooling water comes out from it.
  4. Infuse the cooling water again up to the level where it is about to come out from the radiator cap. Then close the cap.
  5. Run the engine until the valve of the thermostat opens.
  6. Stop the engine. You can confirm it has done by touching the hose between the engine and the radiator and feeling the warm water is running. * Before touching the hose, make sure that engine has completely stopped.
  7. After the engine cools down to 50 degrees centigrade or below, open the radiator cap and check the water level.
  8. Supplement the water again up to the level where it is about to come out from the radiator cap if the water level has got lower
  9. If there is a reservoir tank, supplement the cooling water up to the level of "MAX" line after the engine cools down.
