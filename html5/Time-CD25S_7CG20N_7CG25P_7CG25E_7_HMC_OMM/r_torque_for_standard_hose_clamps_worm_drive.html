Torque for Standard Hose Clamps – Worm Drive

Notice:

The chart below gives the torques for initial installation of hose clamps on new hose and for reassembly or retightening of hose clamps on existing hose.

Clamp Width Initial Installation Torque On New Hose
N•m1 lb•in
16 mm (.625 in) 7.5 ± 0.5 65 ± 5
13.5 mm (.531 in) 4.5 ± 0.5 40 ± 5
8 mm (.312 in) 0.9 ± 0.2 8 ± 2

N•m1 lb•in
