Fuel Trim Valve(FTV) Inspection (HMC2.4L Engine Only)

  1. Visually inspect the Fuel trim valve for abrasions or cracking. Replace as necessary.
  2. To ensure the valve is not leaking a blow-by test can be performed.
  3. With the engine off, disconnect the electrical connector to the FTV.
  4. Disconnect the vacuum lines form the FTV to the pressure regulator/converter, at the converterâ€™s tee connection.
  5. Lightly blow through the vacuum line connected to the FTV. Air should not pass through the FTV when de-energized. If air leaks past the FTV when de-energized, replace the FTV.
