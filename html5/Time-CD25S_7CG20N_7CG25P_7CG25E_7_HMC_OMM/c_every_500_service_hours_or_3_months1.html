Every 500 Service Hours or 3 Months

You must read and understand the warnings and instructions contained in the Safety section of this manual, before performing any operation or maintenance procedures.

  • Engine Oil & Filter – Change
  • Belts – Check, Adjust
  • Mast Hinge Pin – Lubricate
  • Mast, Carriage, Lift Chains & Attachments – Inspect, Lubricate
  • Transmission Oil Filter – Change
  • Overhead Guard – Inspect
  • Drive Axle Oil & Strainer – Change, Clean
  • Steering Mechanism – Check, Lubricate
  • Steer Suspension – Inspect
  • Parking Brake – Test, Adjust
  • Inching & Braking Control Shaft – Lubricate
  • Horn and Lights (If Equipped) – Check
  • Fuel Filter (CD20/25/30/33S-7, CD35C-7 D24NAP Engine only) – Change
  • Engine Intake/Exhaust Valve Clearance – Adjustment (GCT GK25 Engine Only)
  • Inspect Vacuum Lines and Fittings (GCT GK25/HMC2.4L Engine Only)
  • Inspect Electrical System (LP, Dual Fuel Engine Only)
  • Fuel Trim Valve(FTV) Inspection (HMC2.4L Engine Only)
  • Fuel Filter (CD20/25/30/33S-7, CD35C-7 Yanmar Engine Only) – Change
  • Governor Lever and Engine Speed Control (CD20/25/30/33S-7, CD35C-7 Yanmar Engine Only) – Check, Adjust
