Place a container under the engine for collecting drained oil.

Open the drain plug (Figure 5-5,(1)) and drain the engine oil. Remove the oil refill cap (Figure 5-5,(2)).

Using the oil filter cap (110910-00628), replace the oil filter (Figure 5-6,(1)).

Use the genuine engine oil approved / recommended by the manufacturer.

Close the oil refill cap (Figure 5-5,(2)) and install the oil level gauge.

Run the engine for 5 minutes to check engine oil leak, and engine oil level with the oil level gauge.

Notice:

Servicing of the engine oil and oil filter element will largely affect the engine performance as well as the engine life.

Engine oil and filter element must be changed after the first 50 hours.
