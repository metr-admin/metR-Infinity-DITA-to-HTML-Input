421

EST1_Fault

EST1 output open, possibly open EST1 signal or defective spark module
  1. TurnOnMIL
  2. CheckEngineLight

Check coil driver wiring and connector for open circuit.

SECM Pin#6 (EST1) to OEM ignition system. See application note.

Verify GND on ignition module Pin A (of both connectors).

Verify +12 Vdc on ignition module Pin B (of both connectors).
