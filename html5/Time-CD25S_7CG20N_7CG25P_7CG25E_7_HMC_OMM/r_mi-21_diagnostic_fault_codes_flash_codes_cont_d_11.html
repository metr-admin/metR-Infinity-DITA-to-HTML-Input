LSDFault_Dither1

  3. CheckEngineLight
  4. DisableGasPost O2Ctrl (Certified Units Only)

FTV1 Pin 1 (signal) to SECM Pin#7

FTV1 Pin 2 (power) to SECM (DRVP) Pin#67

Check FTV1 for an open coil by disconnecting the FTV connector and measuring the resistance (17.2Ω ± 2Ω @ 23 ± 5°C)

716

LSDFault_FuelPump

Fuel pump fault, signal has opened, shorted to ground or power, or defective fuel pump
  1. TurnOnMIL
  2. CheckEngineLight

Check fuel pump for an open wire or connector being disconnected or signal shorted to GND.

Fuel Pump Pin B (signal) from Engine Ground SECM Pin#69,70

Fuel Pump Pin A (power) from relay4 output

Fuel Pump Relay ground to SECM Pin#20

Fuel Pump Relay power to DRVP Pin#67 of Main relay2

Check Fuel Pump for an open coil by disconnecting the Fuel Pump connector and measuring the resistance.

Check for 12V to fuel pump.
