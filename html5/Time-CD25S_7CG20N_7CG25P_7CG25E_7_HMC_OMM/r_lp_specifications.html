LP Specifications

LP is “liquefied petroleum gas”. The exact composition of LP varies slightly between different parts of the country and different refineries. HD5 or HD10 is recommended for Time forklift trucks.

Composition of HD5
Propane (C3 H8) 90.0 %
Propylene up to 5 %
Butane (C4 H10) 2.0 %
iso - Butane 1.5 %
Methane (CH4) 1.5 %
Total 100 %
Warning:

Remember LP is heavier than air and will sink to the lowest spot possible. Avoid areas near floor drains or lubrication pits where escaped fuel may collect.
