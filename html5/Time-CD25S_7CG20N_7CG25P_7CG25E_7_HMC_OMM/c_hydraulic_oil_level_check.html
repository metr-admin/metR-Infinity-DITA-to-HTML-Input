Hydraulic Oil Level – Check

Warning:

At operating temperature, the hydraulic tank is hot and under pressure.

Hot oil can cause burns.

Remove the filter cap only when the engine is stopped, and the cap is cool enough to touch with your bare hand. Remove the filter cap slowly to relieve pressure.

Operate the lift truck for a few minutes to warm the oil. Park the lift truck on a level surface, with the forks lowered, mast tilted back, parking brake engaged, transmission in NEUTRAL and the engine stopped.

Raise the hood and seat assembly. Make sure the air lift cylinder securely holds the hood open.

Remove the dip stick/filter cap. Maintain the oil level to the FULL mark on the dip stick/filter cap.
