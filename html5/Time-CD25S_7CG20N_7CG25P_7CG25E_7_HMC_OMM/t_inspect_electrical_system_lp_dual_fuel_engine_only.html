Inspect Electrical System (LP, Dual Fuel Engine Only)

  1. Check for loose, dirty or damaged connectors and wires on the harness including: Fuel lock-off, TMAP sensor, O2 sensor, Electronic throttle, Control Relays, Fuel Trim Valve, Foot Pedal, and crank sensor.
  2. Repair and/or replace as necessary.
