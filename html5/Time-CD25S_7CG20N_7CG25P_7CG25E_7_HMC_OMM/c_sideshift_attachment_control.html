Sideshift Attachment Control

The side shift attachment controller is at the front-right side of the driver, and the side shift control lever is at the right as shown in the figure.

Side shift left (1) – open the knob softly forward to move the carriage to the left.

Side shift stop (2) – release the side shift knob. The knob will return to HOLD position and the side shifting operation will stop.

Side shift right (3) – pull the knob softly forward reverse to move the carriage to the right.

Note:

To prevent sudden position shift of the load, operate the lift, tilting and side shift controller smoothly.
