Drive Wheel Bearing (Power Shaft and Drive Wheel) – Apply Grease and Reassemble

Park the lift truck level with the forks lowered, parking brake engaged, transmission in NEUTRAL and the engine stopped.

  1. Lift the drive wheels off the ground. Place stands or blocking under the frame and the mast to support the lift truck. Loosen the wheel mounting nuts(1) and remove the drive wheel assembly.
  2. Loosen the adapter assembly bolts(1) and remove the disk brake adapter(2).
  3. Straighten the lock washer tangs.
    • Loosen the wheel bearing nut(1). Remove the lock washer(2), grease shield plate(3) and wheel bearing cone(4).

    Observe the grease of the wheel hub inside. Add grease if necessary.

    Wheel bearing reassembling

    Consult your Time lift truck branch for the proper wheel bearing reassembling procedure. Packing of bearings and adjustment procedure must be made by a trained mechanic or branch personnel.
