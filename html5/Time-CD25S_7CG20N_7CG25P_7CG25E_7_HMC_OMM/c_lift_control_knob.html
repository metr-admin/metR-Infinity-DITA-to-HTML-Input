Lift Control knob

Lower – Push the knob forward smoothly to lower the lift forks.

Hold – Release the lift knob. The knob will return to the centre(hold) position and the forks will remain in the position they are in.

Raise – Pull the knob back smoothly to raise the lift forks.

Note:
To prevent a sudden change of position of the load, operate all lift, tilt and attachment knobs smoothly.
