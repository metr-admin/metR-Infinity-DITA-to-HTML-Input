Mast Chain Adjustment – FF, FFT Mast

Typical example for FF mast

Typical example for FFT mast

Make sure that mast height is correct. If correct, adjust chain for equal tension. If not, adjust mast chain for correct mast height by adjusting anchor nuts (3), (4).

See the previous section, “Carriage Roller Extrusion” in “When Required” for proper inner mast height.

  1. Lift the inner mast and put blocks under the inner mast to release the tension from the lift chains.
  2. Loosen nut (3) and adjust nut (4) to make inner mast rail flush with outer mast rail bottom.
  3. Make adjustment anchor nuts (3), (4) for equal chain tension.
  4. Raise the inner mast and check equal chain tension. If not equal, repeat the same procedure as step 1 through step 3.
  5. Put LOCTITE No. 242 tread lock on the threads of the anchor nuts (3), (4) after the adjustment is completed.
