Engine Oil (DEO and EO)

The following oil specifications provide guidelines for the selection of commercial products:

  • Gasoline/LP Engine: API SJ or higher

Engine Oil Service hours can be extended to 500 hours by using Time supplied specific oil. Please consult Time branch about it.

  • Diesel Engine:

API CI4, ACEA E5 or higher (4TNE 98 & 4TNE94L T3)

API CJ4, ACEA E9 or higher (D24NAP T4)

Notice:

Failure to follow the oil recommendations can cause shortened engine lift due to carbon deposits or excessive wear. Especially for D24 TIER4 FINAL engine, API CJ4 (ACEA E9) engine oil should be used, because of EGR & DOC performance.

Consult the EMA Lubricating Oils Data Book for a listing of oil brands.

The percentage of sulphur in the fuel will affect the engine oil recommendations.

For fuel sulphur effects, the Infrared Analysis or the ASTM D2896 procedure can be used to evaluate the residual neutralisation properties of engine oil. The sulphur products formation depends on the fuel sulphur content, oil formulation, crankcase blow-by, engine operating conditions and ambient temperature.
