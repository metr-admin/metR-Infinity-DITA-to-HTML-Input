Oil Cooled Disc Brake Only

Use heavy duty hydraulic brake fluid certified by oil supplier to meet the latest version of following classifications.

  • ISO 6743/4 HM
  • AFNOR NFE 48-603 HM
  • DIN 51524 TEIL 2 H-LP
  • HAGGLUNDS DENISON HFO-HF2
  • CINCINNATI P68,69,70

Viscosity: ISO VG32

Brake reservoir oils that have passed the Vickers vane pump test (35VQ25). These oils should have antiwear, antifoam, antirust and antioxidation additives for heavy duty use as stated by the oil supplier. ISO viscosity grade of 32 would normally be selected.
