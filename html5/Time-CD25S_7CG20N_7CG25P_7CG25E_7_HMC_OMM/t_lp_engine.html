LP Engine

Warning:

LP fuel is flammable and can cause personal injury.

Inspect LP fuel lines and fitting for leaks.

Inspect tank for secure mounting.

  1. Turn the tank fuel valve ON by slowly turning the valve counterclockwise. Observe the LP gauge (if equipped).
  2. Don’t press accelerator pedal, and turn the ignition switch to the START position.
  3. Once the engine starts, release the ignition switch.
  4. If the engine does not start, repeat step 2.
  5. If engine coolant is cold, engine speed could be higher than normal low idle speed. Don’t drive forklift until engine speed becomes normal low idle speed.
