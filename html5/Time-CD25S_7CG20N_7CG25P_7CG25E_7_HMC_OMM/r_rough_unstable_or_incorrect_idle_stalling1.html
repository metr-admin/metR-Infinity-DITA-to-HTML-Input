ROUGH, UNSTABLE, OR INCORRECT IDLE, STALLING

Checks Action
Engine Mechanical Check

Check the engine for:

  • Broken motor mounts.
  • Improper valve timing.
  • Low compression.
  • Improper valve clearance.
  • Worn rocker arms.
  • Broken or weak valve springs.
  • Worn camshaft lobes.
