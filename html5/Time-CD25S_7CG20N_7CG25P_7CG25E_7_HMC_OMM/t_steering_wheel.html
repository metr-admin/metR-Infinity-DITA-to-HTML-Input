Steering Wheel

  1. Raise Forks 3 to 6 in (76 to 152 mm) from Floor.
  2. Place Wheel Chocks under Both Drive Wheels.
  3. Locate Hydraulic Jack under Steering Axle as Shown in Figure A.
  4. Jack Up Truck with Hydraulic Jack.
  5. Set Jack Stand Height as Required Not to Exceed 16 in (405mm).
