Replace Spark Plugs (LP, Dual Fuel Engine Only)

  1. Disconnect Battery Cables.
  2. Using a gentle twisting motion remove the high voltage cables from the spark plugs. Replace any damaged cables.
  3. Remove the spark plugs.
  4. Gap the new spark plugs to the proper specifications.

    GCT GK25 Engine: 0.8~0.9 mm

    G424P(E)/HMC2.4L Engine: 0.9 mm

  5. Apply anti-seize compound to the spark plug threads and install.

    GCT GK25 Engine: 19.6 N•m (14 lb•ft)

    G424P(E)/HMC2.4L Engine: 30 N•m (22 lb•ft)

    Warning:

    DO NOT OVERTIGHTEN THE SPARK PLUGS.

  6. Re-install the high voltage cables.
