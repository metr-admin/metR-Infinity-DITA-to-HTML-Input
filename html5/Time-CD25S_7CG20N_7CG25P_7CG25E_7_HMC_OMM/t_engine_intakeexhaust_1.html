Engine Intake/Exhaust Valve Clearance – Adjustment (GCT GK25 Engine Only)

Warning:

Adjustment must be made with the vehicle in hot condition.

  1. Warm up engine until engine coolant temperature gauge shows nearly centre, and then stop it.
  2. Remove rocker cover.
  3. Turn the crankshaft.
    Warning:

    Disconnect high-tension cable from ignition coil when rotating crankshaft with starter motor.

  4. Set the piston for the No. 1 cylinder to the compression TDC and adjust the valves of (1), (2), (3), and (5) in the figure 5-10.
  5. Set the piston for the No. 4 cylinder to the compression TDC by rotating crank again and adjust the valves of (4), (6), (7), and (8) in the figure 5-10.
    • Intake valve clearance: 0.38mm (0.015in)
    • Exhaust valve clearance: 0.38mm (0.015in)
