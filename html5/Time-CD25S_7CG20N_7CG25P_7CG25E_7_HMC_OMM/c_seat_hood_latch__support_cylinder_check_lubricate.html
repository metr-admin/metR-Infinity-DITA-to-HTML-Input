Seat, Hood Latch & Support Cylinder – Check, Lubricate

Check the operation of the seat adjuster rod. Make sure that the seat slides freely on its track.

Lightly oil the seat slider tracks if necessary.

Push the lever down to raise the hood and seat assembly. Make certain the support cylinder will hold the hood open.

(Note: Unlock latch before pulling it – if key equipped)

Lightly oil the hood latch mechanism and the rod for the hood support cylinder.
