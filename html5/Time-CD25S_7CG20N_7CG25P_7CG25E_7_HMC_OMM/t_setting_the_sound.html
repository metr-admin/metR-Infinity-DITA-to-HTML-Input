Setting the sound

  1. Press MENU button repeatedly to select the Sound setting mode as below:
      • BAS (Bass): sets the bass sound level. (-5 ~ +5)
      • MID (Middle): sets the middle sound level. (-5 ~ +5)
      • TRE (Treble): sets the treble sound level. (-5 ~ +5)
      • FAD (Fader: Option): sets the sound fade between the front and rear speakers. (F15 ~ R15)
      • BAL (Balance): sets the sound balance between the right and left speakers. (L 15 ~ R15)
      • LOUD (Loudness): turn Loudness mode on/off
      • EQ (Equalizer style): selects the one of the 7 EQ styles (EQ OFF, POP, ROCK, COUNTRY, VOICE, JAZZ, CLASSIC)
      • BEEP (Beep): turn Beep sound on/off
      • SCROLL (Scroll): turn Scroll mode on/off
      • VOL (Volume): sets the sound volume level. (VOL 0 ~ VOL 41)
  2. Turn the VOLUME dial left/right to adjust the value of the level, balance or style.
