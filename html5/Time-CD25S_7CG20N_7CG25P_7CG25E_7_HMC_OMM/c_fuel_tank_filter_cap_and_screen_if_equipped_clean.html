Fuel Tank Filter Cap and Screen (If Equipped) – Clean

Park the lift truck with the forks lowered, parking brake applied, transmission in neutral and the engine stopped.

Remove the filter cap assembly. Clean in clean, nonflammable solvent.

Dry cap assembly.

Install filter cap assembly.

Warning:

Fuel leaked or spilled onto hot surfaces or electrical components can cause a fire.

Drain moisture and sediment from fuel tank as required by prevailing conditions.
