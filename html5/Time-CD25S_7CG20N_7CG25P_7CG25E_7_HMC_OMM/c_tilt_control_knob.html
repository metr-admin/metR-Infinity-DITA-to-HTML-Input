Tilt Control knob

Tilt Forward – Push the knob forward smoothly to tilt the lift forks forward.

Hold – Release the tilt knob. The knob will return to the centre(hold) position and the forks will remain in the position they are in.

Tilt Back – Pull the knob back smoothly to tilt the lift forks back.

Note:
To prevent a sudden change of position of the load, operate all lift, tilt and attachment knobs smoothly.
