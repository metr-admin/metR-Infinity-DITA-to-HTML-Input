Drive Axle Oil & Strainer – Change, Clean

Park the lift truck level, with the forks lowered, parking brake engaged, transmission in NEUTRAL and engine stopped.

Remove drain plug. Allow the oil to drain into a suitable container. Clean the magnetic drain plug. Check O-ring seal and replace if necessary.

Install the drain plug.

Wash the strainer assembly in clean, non flammable solvent and dry it. Install the strainer assembly and reconnect the hose.

Remove the dip stick/filter cap. Fill the drive axle housing with oil.

See “Refill Capacities.”

Start the lift truck with engine at low idle, place the directional control lever to the NEUTRAL.

Maintain the oil level between lower mark and upper mark on the dip stick/filter cap.

Install the dip stick/filter cap.
