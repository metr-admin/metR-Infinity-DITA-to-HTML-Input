Drive Axle Oil

Do not use Gear Oil in the final drives or differentials. Gear Oil can cause seal material to fail and possibly leak oil.

Failure to follow the recommendation will cause shortened life due to excessive gear wear.

The API CD/TO-2 specification or MIL-L-2104D, Eor F oils could be used.

Multi-grade oils are not blended by Time for use in transmissions. Multi-grade oils which use high molecular weight polymers as viscosity index improvers lose their viscosity effectiveness by permanent and temporary shear of the viscosity index improver and therefore, are not recommended for transmission and drive train compartments.
