Ground Speed Control (CD20/25/30/33S-7, CD35C-7 D24NAP Engine ONLY)

The following describes how to perform ground speed control using the instrument panel:

This method is available in vehicles configured with CAN communication.

Front view

Rear view with an indication of the control switch

Switch at notch 0

Ground speed not limited

Switch at notch 1

Ground speed limited to 8 km/h

Switch at notch 2

Ground speed limited to 10km/h

Switch at notch 3

Ground speed limited to 15km/h

*Notches 4 and 5 are not used.
