Inspect Ignition System (LP, Dual Fuel Engine Only)

  1. Disconnect Battery Cables.
  2. Remove and inspect the spark plugs. Replace as required.
  3. Test secondary cables with an Ohmmeter. If the maximum resistance is higher than 25 kOhms, repair and/or replace(G424F(E) engine only).
  4. Inspect the ignition coil for cracks and heat deterioration. Visually inspect the coil heat sink fins. If any fins are broken replace as required.
  • GCT GK25 Engine
  • G424P(E) Engine
  • HMC2.4L Engine
