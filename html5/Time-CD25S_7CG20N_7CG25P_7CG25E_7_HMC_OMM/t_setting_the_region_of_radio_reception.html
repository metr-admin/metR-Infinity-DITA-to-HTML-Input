Setting the region of radio reception

  1. When the power is turned on, press and hold the buttons more 3 seconds at the same time as below:
    • U.S.A: Hold down and press more 3 seconds

      FM: 87.7 – 107.9 MHz (200 kHz step)

      AM: 530 – 1.7 10 kHz (10kHz step)

    • South America: Hold down and press more 3 seconds

      FM: 87.5 – 108.0 MHz (100 kHz step)

      AM: 530 – 1.7 10 kHz (10kHz step)

    • Asia: Hold down and press more 3 seconds

      FM: 87.5 – 108.0 MHz (100 kHz step)

      AM: 531 – 1,602 kHz (9 kHz step)

    • Europe: Hold down and press seconds

      FM: 87.5 – 108.0 MHz (50 kHz step)

      AM: 522 – 1,629 kHz (9 kHz step)

  2. Please wait for more 5 seconds with no operation, the unit will save and apply your's setting.

    If the region setting is not selected correctly to your country or region, the radio reception can not be received. Retry the setting the region of radio reception correctly.

    The region setting is required only for the first time

    The region setting is return to the default setting when the power connector or battery is disconnected.
