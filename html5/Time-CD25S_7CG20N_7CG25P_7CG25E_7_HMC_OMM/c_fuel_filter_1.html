Fuel Filter (CD20/25/30/33S-7, CD35C-7 D24NAP Engine only) – Change

The fuel filter element must be replaced on a regular basis to prevent foreign materials from entering the diesel fuel system.

Stop and cool down the engine.

Disconnect wire harness connector (A) and disconnect the quick connector (C) connected to the fuel filter port. (Figure 5-7)

Remove the screw (B) to separate the fuel filter cap. When removing the fuel filter, hold it carefully to prevent fuel from flowing out. Wipe and clean spilled fuel.

Install a new fuel filter and assemble the fuel filter cap. Assemble the fuel filter cap by inserting the steel pipe (A) under the fuel filter cap in the O-ring (B) of the filter element. (Figure 5-8)

Connect the wire harness connector and quick connector. When connecting the quick connector, take care to prevent foreign material from entering the hose.

After replacing the fuel filter or element, or having exhausted fuel, it is necessary to remove the air in the fuel system for safe engine start up.

Turn priming pump(A) into UNLOCK direction to protrude.

And press the protruded priming pump with hand and continue pumping until air becomes vacuum and fuel comes out of priming pump.

After air becomes vacuous, place priming pump to original place, LOCK direction. Refer to “Priming the Fuel System – as necessary”. (Figure 5-9)

Check the fuel leakage.
