Every 2000 Service Hours or Yearly

You must read and understand the warnings and instructions contained in the Safety section of this manual, before performing any operation or maintenance procedure.

  • Engine Valve Lash (Diesel Engine Only) – Check, Adjust
  • Steer Wheel Bearings – Reassemble
  • Drive Wheel Bearing (Power Shaft and Drive Wheel) – Apply Grease and Reassemble
  • Cooling System – Clean, Change
  • Forks – Inspect
