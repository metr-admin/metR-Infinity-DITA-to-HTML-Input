Tyre Inflation

Warning:

Personal injury or death could result when tyres are inflated incorrectly.

Use a self-attaching inflation chuck and stand behind the tread when inflating a tyre.

Proper inflation equipment and training in using the equipment are necessary to avoid over-inflation. A tyre blowout or rim failure can result from improper or misused equipment.

Notice:

Set the tyre inflation equipment regulator at no more than 140 kPa (20 psi) over the recommended tyre pressure.
