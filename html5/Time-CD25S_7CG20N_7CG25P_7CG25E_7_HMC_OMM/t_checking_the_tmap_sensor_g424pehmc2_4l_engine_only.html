Checking the TMAP Sensor (G424P(E),HMC2.4L Engine Only)

  1. Verify that the TMAP sensor (F) is mounted tightly into the manifold adapter (E), with no leakage.
  2. If the TMAP is found to be loose, remove the TMAP retaining screw and the TMAP sensor from the manifold adapter.
  3. Visually inspect the TMAP O-ring seal for damage. Replace as necessary.
  4. Apply a thin coat of an approved silicon lubricant to the TMAP o-ring seal.
  5. Re-install the TMAP sensor into the manifold adapter and securely tighten the retaining screw.
