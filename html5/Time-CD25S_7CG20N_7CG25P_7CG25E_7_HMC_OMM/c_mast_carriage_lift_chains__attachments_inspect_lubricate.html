Mast, Carriage, Lift Chains & Attachments – Inspect, Lubricate

Operate the lift, tilt and attachment controls. Listen for unusual noises. These may indicate a need for repair.

Inspect for loose bolts and nuts on the carriage.

Remove any debris from the carriage and mast.

Inspect the forks and attachments for free operation and damage. Have repairs made if necessary.

Brush a film of oil on all links of the chain.

Raise and lower the carriage a few times to allow lubricant to enter into the chain links.

Notice:

Lubricate chains more frequently than normal in applications where the lift truck is operating in an atmosphere which could cause corrosion of components or when lift truck must work in rapid lift cycles.

Inspect the chain anchors and individual links for wear, loose pins or cracked leaves.
