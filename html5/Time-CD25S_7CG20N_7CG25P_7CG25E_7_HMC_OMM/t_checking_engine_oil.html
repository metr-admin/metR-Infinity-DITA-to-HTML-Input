Checking Engine Oil

  1. Check that the engine is at level position.
  2. Start and warm up the engine to operating temperature.
  3. Stop the engine and wait for 5~10 minutes.
  4. Pull out the oil level gauge (dip stick) (Figure 5-3,(1)) upward.
  5. Wipe the scale of the oil level gauge with a clean cloth.
  6. Insert the oil level gauge (Figure 5-3(1)) into the coil level check hole.
  7. Pull out the oil level gauge again to check the oil level.
