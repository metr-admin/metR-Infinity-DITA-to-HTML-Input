Ready to get started?

Log into the Power Customer Community with your credentials. Not yet registered? No problem. Set up an account to receive your credentials via our registration page.

After you log in, explore everything the community has to offer by clicking the “Get started” tab in the top navigation menu.

If you’re ready to jump right into training, there are two ways to access the Power Learning Center:

  • On the home page, click the “Learning center” tile
  • In the top navigation, click the “Learn” tab and then click “Learning center” on the dropdown menu
