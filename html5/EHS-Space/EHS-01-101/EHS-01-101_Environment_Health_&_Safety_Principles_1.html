Environment, Health & Safety Principles

  1. EHS is everyone’s responsibility as an employee of Space: EHS will be led from the top down and owned at every level throughout the organization on the basis of MUTUAL TRUST and appreciation.
  2. EHS will always look for resolution instead of blame in the incident/injury resolution process.
  3. EHS pledges to be Simple, Practical, Effective, and Swift in response to customer needs in the incident/injury resolution process.
    1. Definition of customers:
      1. Employees
      2. Visitors
      3. Contractors
      4. Stakeholders within and outside of the Space family
    2. Definition of focus: mitigation and/or elimination of risk to
      1. People
      2. Strategy
      3. Execution

Scope

All sites within the Space organization in all regions are expected to develop site specific EHS procedures with these Global Environment, Health & Safety Principles in mind.

Purpose

  1. EHS Principles are the basis for the Life Saving Protocols that have been developed to ensure personnel execute basic actions that prevent incidents, injuries and losses caused by common glass industry activities.
  2. EHS Principles are also the basis for the “Golden Rules” that complement the Life Saving Control Protocols listed within.

References
