ENVIRONMENT HEALTH SAFETY POLICY

VISION AND VALUES

Space is committed to the health, safety and well-being of our employees and stakeholders. Our passion for people safely leading performance, along with our Diversity, Equity and Inclusion values, drive us to provide a safe and healthy workplace for our employees, contractors, and visitors so we can finish each and every day injury and incident free. We will be the most innovative, sustainable, and chosen supplier of brand-building packaging solutions, while being a responsible steward of the environment by integrating sustainable business practices into our daily business decisions.

SCOPE APPLICABILITY

This policy applies to all Space employees and, to the extent applicable, contractors, suppliers and visitors.

ENVIRONMENT HEALTH AND SAFETY

Our commitment to the environment, health and safety is integral to our Global Code of Conduct and is reflected in our Company values and sustainability goals. All Space employees are responsible to act in a manner that advances us on our journey to environmental, health and safety excellence through reducing injuries and illnesses, minimizing our impact on the environment, and protecting natural resources. We are committed to meeting or exceeding all environmental, health and safety laws and regulations. The Space Global Leadership Team will lead by example while providing guidance to support the Company's purpose and vision through key programs such as Space CARES, which focus on an integrated culture with high employee and management engagement.

Space Leadership Goals

  • Build collaborative relationships, communicate with authenticity, model and demand accountability.
  • Communicate our environmental, health and safety performance and actively seek input from all stakeholders as we drive towards continuous improvement.
  • Develop people and teams to promote a proactive safety culture of active caring and to develop leading key performance indicators.
  • Demonstrate enterprise leadership and make sound decisions considering the health, safety and environmental impact in every business decision.
  • Create a safe and healthy workplace by partnering with employees, contractors, suppliers and visitors to identify and control workplace hazards.
  • Reduce the environmental impact of our operations through our sustainability initiatives: increase pollution-prevention practices; reduce waste/increase recycling; reduce wastewater generation and minimize the consumption of natural resources (raw materials, water and energy) while lowering greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Develop and execute communication and training strategies to ensure all employees understand their environment, health, and safety responsibilities to themselves and each other, as a critical element of actively caring for people.

Employee Goals

  • Demonstrate active care through a genuine commitment to their own and each other's safety and wellbeing.
  • Engage with our Space Safety Defenses and embrace Walks and Talks to drive expected behaviors, identify hazards to improve the workplace, and report all near misses to allow necessary measures to be taken to prevent incidents.
  • Comply with all Company environment, health and safety policies and work-rules.
  • Adhere to our Golden Rules for Safety and actively promote them in our day-to-day work activities.
  • Start routine or non-routine activities only after a risk analysis is executed and controls are in place.
  • Leverage Stop Work Authority to avoid injuries in the workplace.
