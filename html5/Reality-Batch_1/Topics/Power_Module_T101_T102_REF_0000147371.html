Power Block

Reality NAM

One power module consists of:

  • 3 rectifiers (PFC and step-up)

  • 3 inverters

  • 1 battery charger (step down)

  • 1 RFI board

  • 1 PSU board

  • 1 current limiter and filter board

Three individual rectifiers and three individual inverters form one power module with a 12.5 kVA rating. Each PFC rectifier and inverter is connected in one common single phase board. For a 3-phase output configuration each line (phase) has its own board.

3-Phase Output System

One power module consists of three individual inverters each of which deliver 4.17 kVA with a phase shift of 120°. In a 3-phase configuration with one power module (12.5 kVA System), the individual output line delivers 4.17 kVA.
