Connect the Power Cables in Bottom Cable Entry System

  1. Connect the PE cables to the PE busbar in the bottom of the UPS.
  2. Connect the power cables:
    1. Connect the input cables to the X001 (L1, L2, L3, N1) terminals.
    2. Connect the bypass cables to the X501 terminals (3:3: L1, L2, L3, N)/(3:1: L, N).
    3. Connect the output cables to the X601 terminals (3:3: L1, L2, L3, N)/(3:1: L, N).
    4. Connect the battery cables to the X201 (B+,B-) terminals.
  3. Fasten the cables to the panel in the bottom of the UPS using cable reliefs.
1 No N if input transformer is installed
