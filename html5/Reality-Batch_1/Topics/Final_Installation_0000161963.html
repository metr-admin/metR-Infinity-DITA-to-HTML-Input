Final Installation Steps

Reality NAM UPS with transformer 25–100 kVA

  1. Reinstall all covers and plates on the cabinet(s).
  2. Only for seismic anchoring: Install the front seismic anchoring bracket on the cabinet(s).
  3. Reattach the kick plates to the spring clips on the cabinet(s).
  4. Fasten the kick plates with the M5 screws.
