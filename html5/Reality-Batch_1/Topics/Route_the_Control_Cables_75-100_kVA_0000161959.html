Route the Signal Cables

Reality NAM UPS with transformer 75–100 kVA

Note: Battery cables and signal cables must be routed separately to reduce noise.
  1. Remove the plate in the bottom left side of the power section of the UPS cabinet.
  2. Remove the cable channel cover in the power section of the UPS cabinet.
  3. Route the signal cables through the top or bottom of the I/O section of the UPS cabinet to the control terminal block TB1.
  4. Only in systems with adjacent battery cabinet(s): Route the signal cables from the adjacent battery cabinet(s) through the cable channel in the power section of the UPS cabinet to the control terminal block TB1 in the I/O section of the UPS cabinet as shown.
  5. Route the signal cables through the top or bottom of the I/O section of the UPS cabinet through the cable channel in the power section of the UPS cabinet to the external connection board 0P2553.
