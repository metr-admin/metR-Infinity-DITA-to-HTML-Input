Connect the Power Cables on the 10-40 kVA Parallel Redundant System with Top Cable Entry and Bypass Transformer

  1. Route the power cables through the top of the AUX cabinets.
  2. Connect the PE cables to the PE busbar in the top of the AUX cabinets.
  3. Connect the power cables:
    1. Connect the input cables to the X311 (L1, L2, L3, N1) terminals in AUX cabinet 1 and AUX cabinet 2.
    2. Connect the bypass cables to the X301 (3:3: L1, L2, L3)/(3:1 10–20 kVA: L, N)/(3:1 30–40 kVA: L1, L2) terminals in AUX cabinet 2.
    3. Connect the output cables to the X699 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) terminals in AUX cabinet 2.
    4. Connect the output interconnection cables from the X692 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) terminals in AUX cabinet 1 to X692 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) in AUX cabinet 2.
    5. Connect the bypass transformer interconnection cables from X312 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) in AUX cabinet 1 to X312 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) in AUX cabinet 2.
    6. Connect the battery cables to the X314 (B+,B-) terminals in AUX cabinet 1 and AUX cabinet 2.
1 No N if input transformer is installed
