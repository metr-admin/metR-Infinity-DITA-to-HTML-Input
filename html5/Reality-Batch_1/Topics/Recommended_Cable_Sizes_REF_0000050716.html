Recommended Cable Sizes

DANGER
hazard of electric shock, explosion, or arc flash
Use power cables of equal resistance, equal length, and equal size for the same function.
Note: The maximum cable length is 100 meters.

Cable sizes in this manual are based on table 52–C2 of IEC 60364–5–52 with the following assertions:

  • 90 °C conductors

  • Use of copper conductors

  • An ambient temperature of 30 °C

  • 400 V/230 V and grouping of four cables

If the ambient temperature is greater than 30 °C, larger conductors are to be selected with the correction factors of the IEC.
