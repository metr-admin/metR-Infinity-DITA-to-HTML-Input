Measure for Voltages and Currents

Reality EMEA

Use a probe to measure for voltages and currents at the terminals/busbars — refer to the installation manual 990–5729 (single UPS and parallel UPS system without coupling switches) or 990–6071 (parallel UPS system with coupling switches) to see access to and placement of busbars and connection of power cables. Note that some systems contains input, bypass, and output transformers.
