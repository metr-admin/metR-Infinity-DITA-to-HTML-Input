Power Block Principle Diagram

One power block consists of:

  • Power module (maximum 2)

  • Controller box

  • Power connection board

  • Capacitor board

  • Fan with fan monitor

Power Block Structure

One power block consists of one or two power modules (max. 40 kVA)

  • Up to two power blocks can be paralleled (max. 80 kVA)

  • Each power block has its own controller (max. 2 controllers)

    • Data collection, communication, load sharing and parallel behavior is controlled by the master controller

  • Inverter self-test is run at every start-up

  • 10 kVA, 30 kVA and 50 kVA are derated

  • Odd number of power modules is allowed
