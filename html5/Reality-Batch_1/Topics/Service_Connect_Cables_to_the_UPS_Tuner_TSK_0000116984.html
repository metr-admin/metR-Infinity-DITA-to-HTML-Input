Connect Cables to the UPS Tuner

Reality EMEA

Connect the serial port on your laptop to the service port on the HMI module (J405) if the HMI module needs service, and to the service port on the controller box (J223) if the power module needs service.
Note: Use the service port cable 940–0024D for the proper connection of all required signals. If you don’t have a serial port on your laptop, you can use a USB-to-serial adaptor. The recommended USB-to-serial adaptors can be found in Service View or by contacting the technical support.
