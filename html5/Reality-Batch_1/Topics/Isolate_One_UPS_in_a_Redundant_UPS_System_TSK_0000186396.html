Isolate One UPS in a Redundant UPS System

Reality EMEA

Note: Check that the other UPS in the redundant UPS system is working correctly with no pending alarms and can supply the load by itself.
  1. Press the ON and the OFF buttons simultaneously on the display. The UPS will shut down with no voltage on the output.
    DANGER
    hazard of electric shock, explosion, or arc flash
    After this operation, power will still be present on the power connection terminals. Ensure that the protection covers are installed.
  2. Open coupling switch Q694 for UPS2 or Q692 for UPS1 (if present).
    DANGER
    hazard of electric shock, explosion, or arc flash
    In a redundant system without coupling switches there will be voltage backfeed from the other UPS when only UPS is switched off for maintenance.
  3. Open switch Q501.
  4. Open battery switch Q201 and/or Q211.
  5. Open switch Q001.
  6. Wait five minutes for the DC capacitors to discharge.
  7. Measure for voltage on all terminals before working on the UPS.
    DANGER
    hazard of electric shock, explosion, or arc flash
    Hazardous voltage is still present on the connecting terminals as input source, bypass source, and batteries are still connected.
