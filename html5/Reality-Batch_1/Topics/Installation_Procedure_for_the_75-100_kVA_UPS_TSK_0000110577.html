Installation Procedure for the 75-100 kVA UPS

Reality NAM 75–100 kVA

Note: The UPS is configured for dual mains as default.
Note: Both top and bottom cable entry is available.
  1. Move the UPS cabinet to the final location with a forklift or a low profile 21 inch pallet jack by lifting from the sides.
  2. Remove the Kick Plates.
  3. Prepare the UPS Cabinet for Cables in Systems with Adjacent Battery Cabinet(s).
  4. Convert the 75-100 kVA UPS from Dual Mains to Single Mains (Option).
  5. Connect the Power Cables on the 75-100 kVA UPS.
  6. Route the Signal Cables.
  7. Connect the Battery Signal Cables.
  8. Connect the Remote Emergency Power Off (REPO).
  9. Connect the Input Contacts and Output Relays (Option).
  10. Reinstall the Kick Plates.
  11. Reinstall all plates and close the doors.
