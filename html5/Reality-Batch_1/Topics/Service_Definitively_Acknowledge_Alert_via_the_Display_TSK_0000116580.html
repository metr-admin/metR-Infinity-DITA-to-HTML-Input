Acknowledge an Alert via the Display

Reality EMEA

Note: This action can only be performed by an FSR. This will definitively acknowledge a pending alert via the display without the need to connect to the service port.
  1. To view the LCM status, select Diagnosis > LCM status. 
    LCM status:Tech. check needed
  2. To definitively acknowledge the pending alert, press the ENTER key on the HMI 5 times within 5 seconds while the alert is shown under LCM status on the screen. The alert is now definitively acknowledged.
