Connect the Power Cables in Top Cable Entry System with Bypass Transformer

  1. Connect the PE cables to the PE busbar.
  2. Connect the power cables:
    1. Connect the input cables to the X001 (L1, L2, L3, N1) terminals.
    2. Connect the bypass cables to the X301 (L1, L2, L3) terminals.
    3. Connect the output cables to the X601 (3:3: L1, L2, L3, N)/(3:1 10–20 kVA: L, N))/(3:1 30–80 kVA: L1, L2) terminals.
    4. Connect the battery cables to the X201 (B+,B-) terminals.
1 No N if input transformer is installed
