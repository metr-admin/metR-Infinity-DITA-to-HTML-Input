Connect the Temperature Sensor

Battery cabinet, GUPXCAB600IEC, GUPXCAB600AIEC, GUPXCAB800IEC, GUPXCAB800AIEC Battery breaker kit, GUPXCD10KIEC, GUPXCD20KIEC, GUPXCD30KIEC, GUPXCD40KIEC, GUPXCD50KIEC, GUPXCD60KIEC, GUPXCD80KIEC Control terminal block kit, GUPXCD00KIEC

  1. Connect the signal cables from the temperature sensor to the control terminal block X011 terminals 1 (red wire), 2 (brown wire), 3 (black wire), and 4 (yellow wire) in the main battery cabinet.
  2. Route the signal cables from the control terminal block X011 terminals 1–4 through the bottom of the main battery cabinet and connect to the external connection board J1603 terminals 1–4 in the UPS cabinet. (This cable is not supplied).
