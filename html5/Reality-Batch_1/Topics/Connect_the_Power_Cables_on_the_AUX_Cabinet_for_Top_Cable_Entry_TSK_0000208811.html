Connect the Power Cables in Top Cable Entry System

  1. Connect the PE cables to the PE busbar in the top of the AUX cabinet.
  2. Connect the power cables:
    1. Connect the input cables to the X311 (L1, L2, L3, N1) terminals.
    2. Connect the bypass cables to the X312 (3:3: L1, L2, L3, N)/(3:1: L, N) terminals.
    3. Connect the output cables to the X313 (3:3: L1, L2, L3, N)/(3:1: L, N) terminals.
    4. Connect the battery cables to the X314 (B+,B-) terminals.
1 No N if input transformer is installed
