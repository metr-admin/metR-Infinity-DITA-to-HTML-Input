Clearance

Battery cabinet, GUPXCAB600IEC, GUPXCAB600AIEC, GUPXCAB800IEC, GUPXCAB800AIEC

The battery cabinet requires a minimum rear clearance of 17 mm. If you place the battery cabinets adjacent to the UPS cabinet, please note that the UPS cabinet requires a minimum rear clearance of 100 mm.

Note: Clearance dimensions are published for airflow only. Consult with the local safety codes and standards for additional requirements in your local area.
