Prepare the Battery Cabinets for Installation

Battery cabinet, GUPXCAB600IEC, GUPXCAB600AIEC, GUPXCAB800IEC, GUPXCAB800AIEC

Note: The main battery cabinet has side panels and a temperature sensor installed. The adjacent battery cabinets have no side panels or temperature sensors installed. The main battery cabinet must always be installed to the left with the adjacent battery cabinet(s) to the right.
  1. Remove the right side panel from the main battery cabinet and install it on the right side of the rightmost adjacent battery cabinet.
  2. Line up the battery cabinets with the main battery cabinet to the left.
  3. Install interconnection brackets on all the battery cabinets. Use C-clamps to hold the battery cabinets together to make the installation easier. Install two interconnection brackets in the front and two interconnection brackets in the rear. Use four screws on each side of each interconnection bracket to fasten it to the battery cabinets.
