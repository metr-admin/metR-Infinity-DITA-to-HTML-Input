Replace the Fan Assembly 0J-0N-96453 (for Transformer)

Reality EMEA 10 kVA 20 kVA 30 KVA 40 kVA

DANGER
hazard of electric shock, explosion, or arc flash
  • Electrical equipment must be installed, operated, serviced, and maintained only by qualified personnel.
  • Apply appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) and follow safe electrical work practices.
  • Disconnection devices for AC and DC must be provided by others, be readily accessible, and the function of the disconnect device marked for its function.
  • Turn off the upstream disconnection devices.
  • Turn off all power supplying the UPS system before working on or inside the equipment.
  • Before working on the UPS system, check for hazardous voltage between all terminals including the protective earth.
  • The UPS contains an internal energy source. Hazardous voltage can be present even when disconnected from the mains supply. Before installing or servicing the UPS system, ensure that the units are OFF and that mains and batteries are disconnected. Wait five minutes before opening the UPS to allow the capacitors to discharge.
  • The UPS must be properly earthed/grounded and due to a high leakage current, the earthing/grounding conductor must be connected first.
Note: This procedure shows the 40 kVA UPS but the procedure is identical for all 10-40 kVA UPS systems.
  1. Remove the screws of the front cover and remove the front cover.
  2. Remove the cover in front of the leftmost fan.
  3. Check that the connectors on the fan speed monitoring board with the black and red wires are correctly labeled and disconnect them.
  4. Remove the two screws from the fan speed monitoring board.
  5. Remove the two hex nuts on the fan assembly bracket and remove the fan assembly bracket.
  6. Slide the leftmost fan out and remove it as far as the wires allow.
  7. Move the other fans to the left and slide them out one by one as far as the wires allow.
  8. From the new fan box, remove the fan speed monitoring board and all four fans by sliding them out of the sheet metal frame. Discard the sheet metal frame.
  9. Replace the old fans with the new fans one by one:
    1. Disconnect the wires from the old fan.
    2. Reconnect the wires to the new fan.
    3. Slide the new fan into position in the fan holder.
  10. Reinstall the fan assembly bracket with the two hex nuts.
  11. Replace the old fan speed monitoring board with the new fan speed monitoring board:
    1. Check that all the DIP switches on the new fan speed monitoring board are set identically to the old fan speed monitoring board.
    2. Reconnect the connectors with the black and red wires to the fan speed monitoring board.
    3. Install the new fan speed monitoring board with the two screws.
  12. Reinstall the cover in front of the leftmost fan.
  13. Reinstall the front cover with the screws.
Check the proper function of the fan assembly by closing the Q001 breaker. Only the green LEDs on the fan speed monitoring board must be on.
