Install the Battery Breaker Kit and Connect the Battery Cables

Battery cabinet, GUPXCAB600IEC, GUPXCAB600AIEC, GUPXCAB800IEC, GUPXCAB800AIEC Battery breaker kit, GUPXCD10KIEC, GUPXCD20KIEC, GUPXCD30KIEC, GUPXCD40KIEC, GUPXCD50KIEC, GUPXCD60KIEC, GUPXCD80KIEC

DANGER
hazard of electric shock, explosion, or arc flash
Perform a total power off of the UPS system before connecting the battery cables to the battery breaker.
DANGER
Hazard of electric shock, explosion, or arc flash
For TT and TN systems each stand alone cabinet of the system must be individually connected to the protective earthing terminal in the distribution board that supplies the system.
Warning
Hazard of arc flash
Use M8 bolts and nuts to connect the power cables.
  1. Remove the left side panel from the main battery cabinet.
  2. Install the battery breaker kit in the dedicated location in the main battery cabinet with the six supplied screws.
  3. Lockout/Tagout the battery breaker.
  4. Remove the four bolts on the protection cover and remove the protection cover.
  5. Route the PE cable through the bottom of the main battery cabinet and connect to the PE busbar.
  6. Route the battery cables (+,-) from the UPS cabinet through the bottom of the main battery cabinet and connect to the battery breaker busbars (+,-).
  7. If adjacent battery cabinets are present: Interconnect the batteries through the sides of the battery cabinets.
  8. Connect the battery cables (+,-) from the batteries to the battery busbars in the rear of the battery breaker kit.
  9. Reinstall the protection cover with the four bolts.
  10. Reinstall the left side panel on the main battery cabinet.
