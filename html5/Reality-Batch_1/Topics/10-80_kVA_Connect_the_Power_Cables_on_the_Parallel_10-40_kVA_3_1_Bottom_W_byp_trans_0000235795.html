Connect the Power Cables on the 50-80 kVA Parallel Redundant System with Bottom Cable Entry and Bypass Transformer

  1. Route the power cables through the bottom of the cabinets.
  2. Connect the PE cables to the PE busbar in the bottom of the cabinets.
  3. Connect the power cables:
    1. Connect the input cables to the X001 (L1, L2, L3, N1) terminals in UPS 1 and UPS 2.
    2. Connect the bypass cables to the X301 (3:3: L1, L2, L3)/(3:1: L1, L2) terminals in the AUX cabinet.
    3. Connect the output cables to the X699 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) terminals in the AUX cabinet.
    4. Connect the output interconnection cables from the X601 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) terminals in UPS 1 to X692 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) in the AUX cabinet.
    5. Connect the bypass transformer interconnection cables from X501 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) in UPS 1 to X312 (3:3 : L1, L2, L3, N)/ (3:1: L, N) in the AUX cabinet.
    6. Connect the battery cables to the X201 (B+,B-) terminals in the UPS 1 and UPS 2.
1 No N if input transformer is installed
