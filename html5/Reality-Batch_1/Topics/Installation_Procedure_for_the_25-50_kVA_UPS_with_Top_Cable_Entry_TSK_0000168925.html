Installation Procedure for the 25–50 kVA UPS with Top Cable Entry

Reality NAM 25–50 kVA UPS

Note: The UPS is configured for dual mains as default.
  1. Move the UPS cabinet and the top entry cabinet to the installation location with a forklift or a low profile 21 inch pallet jack by lifting from the sides.
  2. Remove the Kick Plates.
  3. Install the Top Entry Cabinet (Option).
  4. Only in systems with adjacent battery cabinet(s): Prepare the UPS Cabinet for Cables in Systems with Adjacent Battery Cabinet(s).
  5. Convert the 25-50 kVA UPS from Dual Mains to Single Mains (Option).
  6. Prepare the UPS Cabinet for Cables.
  7. Connect the Power Cables on the 25-50 kVA UPS.
  8. Connect the Battery Signal Cables.
  9. Connect the Remote Emergency Power Off (REPO).
  10. Connect the Input Contacts and Output Relays (Option).
  11. Reinstall the Kick Plates.
  12. Reinstall all plates and close the doors.
