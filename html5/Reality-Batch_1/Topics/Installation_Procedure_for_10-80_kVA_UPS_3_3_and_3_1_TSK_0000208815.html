Installation Procedure for Bottom Cable Entry System

Warning
Tipping hazard
The cabinet is top-heavy. Move the cabinet carefully with a forklift or a low profile pallet jack by lifting from the front.
  1. Remove the Kick Plates.
  2. Mount the UPS System to the Floor.
  3. Prepare for Cables in Bottom Cable Entry System.
  4. Follow one of the procedures:
    • Connect the Power Cables in Bottom Cable Entry System, or
    • Connect the Power Cables in Bottom Cable Entry System with Bypass Transformer.
  5. Connect the Signal Cables.
  6. Reinstall the Kick Plates.
