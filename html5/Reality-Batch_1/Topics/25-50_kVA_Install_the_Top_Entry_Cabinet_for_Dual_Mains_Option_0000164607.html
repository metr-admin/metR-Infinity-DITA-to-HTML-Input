Connect the Power Cables in the Top Entry Cabinet for Dual Mains

Reality NAM UPS with transformer 25–50 kVA Reality top entry cabinet

  1. Route the input cables through the top gland plate and connect to the input busbars (L1, L2, L3, N) and to the ground busbar in the top entry cabinet.
  2. Route the output cables through the top gland plate and connect to the output busbars (L1, L2, L3, N) and to the ground busbar in the top entry cabinet.
  3. Only in systems with remote battery cabinet(s): Route the battery cables from the remote battery cabinet(s) through the top gland plate and connect to the battery busbars (+, –) and the ground busbar in the top entry cabinet
  4. Route the preinstalled input, output, and remote battery cables from the top entry cabinet through the transformer cabinet and into the UPS cabinet as shown.
