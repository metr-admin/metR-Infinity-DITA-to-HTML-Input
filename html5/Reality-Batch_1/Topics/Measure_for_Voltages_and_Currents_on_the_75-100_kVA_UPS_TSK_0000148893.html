Measure for Voltages and Currents on the 75–100 kVA UPS

Reality NAM

  1. Remove the lower front plate in the I/O section.
  2. Connect the equipment grounding conductor to the ground busbar.
  3. Use a probe to measure for voltages and currents at the busbars:
    1. UPS input at input L1, input L2, input L3 , and input N.
    2. Bypass at bypass L1, bypass L2, bypass L3, and bypass N.
    3. UPS output at output L1, output L2, output L3, and output N.
    4. Battery DC at battery + and battery - .
    5. Transformer input at input H1, input H2, input H3, and input N H0.
    6. Transformer bypass at bypass H1, bypass H2, bypass H3, and bypass N H0.
    7. Transformer output at output X1,output X2, output X3, and output N X0.
    DANGER
    HAZARD OF ELECTRIC SHOCK, EXPLOSION, OR ARC FLASH
    Measure voltages and currents on all busbars. The battery busbars for adjacent battery cabinets are placed in the power section of the UPS cabinet.
