Connect the Power Cables in the UPS Cabinet for Single Mains

Reality NAM UPS with transformer 25–50 kVA

Note: Route the battery cables from the adjacent battery cabinet(s) through the left side panel into the UPS cabinet.
  1. Connect the input cables to the input busbars (L1, L2, L3, N) and to the ground busbar.
  2. Connect the output cables to the output busbars (L1, L2, L3, N) and to the ground busbar.
  3. Connect the battery cables to the battery busbars (+, –) and to the ground busbar.
