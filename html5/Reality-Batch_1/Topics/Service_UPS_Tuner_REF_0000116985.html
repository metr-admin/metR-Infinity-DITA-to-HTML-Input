UPS Tuner

Reality EMEA

Connect the UPS Tuner to the service ports on either a controller box or a display module (HMI) to perform critical functions, e.g. firmware upgrades, UPS configuration, global report, logs analysis, and LCM management.

Note: Standard terminal applications like Hyper Terminal or Tera Term cannot be used anymore to perform any of the functions covered by the UPS Tuner.
Note: You need a license to use the UPS Tuner software. See Service View for details about obtaining a license or contact your local Schneider Electric support.
