Detailed Overview of Status LEDs

Reality EMEA

Color Explanation
1 green The rectifier input switch Q001 is closed
red The rectifier input switch Q001 is open
2 green The rectifier mains is within tolerance
red The rectifier mains is outside tolerance
3 green The rectifier is ON
red A rectifier power module alarm is present
4 green The inverter is ON
red An inverter power module alarm is present
5 yellow The inverter is synchronized to the bypass mains
6 green The output is within tolerance
red The output is outside tolerance
7 yellow The battery is charged in float charge mode
8 yellow The battery is charged in boost charge mode or at cyclic charge level mode
9 yellow The battery is charged in initial charge mode
10 green The battery switch Q201 is closed
red The battery switch Q201 is open
11 green The battery voltage is within tolerance
red The battery voltage is outside tolerance
12 green The bypass input switch Q501 is closed
red The bypass input switch Q501 is open
13 green The bypass mains switch Q528 is closed
red The bypass mains switch Q528 is open
14 green The SBS input is within tolerance
red The SBS input is outside tolerance
15 green The static bypass switch Q401 is closed
red The static bypass switch Q401 is open
16 green The manual bypass switch Q601 is in the AUTO position
red The manual bypass switch Q601 is in the BYPASS position
17 green The manual bypass switch Q601 is in the BYPASS position
red The manual bypass switch Q601 is in the AUTO position
