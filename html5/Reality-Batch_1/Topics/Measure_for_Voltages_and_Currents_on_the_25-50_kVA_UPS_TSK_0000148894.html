Measure for Voltages and Currents on the 25–50 kVA UPS

Reality NAM

  1. Remove the plate in the bottom of the UPS cabinet and lift up the second plate that covers the busbars.
  2. Connect the equipment grounding conductor to the ground busbar.
  3. Use a probe to measure for voltages and currents at the busbars:
    1. UPS input at input L1, input L2, input L3 , and input N.
    2. Bypass at bypass L1, bypass L2, bypass L3, and bypass N.
    3. UPS output at output L1, output L2, output L3, and output N.
    4. Battery DC at battery + and battery - .
