Switching the Burner Booster Pumps

Purpose/Application

Switching the Burner Booster Pumps

Follow this procedure to switch the burner cooling water booster pumps:

To switch the burner cooling water booster pumps:
  • Go to the PVAG control panel located behind the Tank 1 & 2 control room.
  • Click on Settings.
  • Click on Pump settings.
  • Click on Change pumps now.
  • One burner cooling water booster pump will automatically turn on when burner cooling water flow drops below the low limit.
Note:

Pumps should be in Automatic mode with automatic switchover enabled during normal operation.
